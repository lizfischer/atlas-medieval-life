HCM 2072
1317, June 4.
ACKNOWLEDGEMENT by John de Aqua blanca, dean, and the chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford of the receipt from domini Hamon de Sandwyco, Richard de Nonnynton', canons of the cathedral church of Hereford, and Robert de la Doune, executors of the will of dominus Stephen de Thansto, formerly dean of Hereford, of twenty-five marks, for which the dean and chapter undertake to pay annually on the obit day of the said Stephen thirteen shillings and four pence to the canons and six shillings and eight pence to the vicars and clerks of the choir present at the obit, in accordance with the wish of the said executors. Given at the chapter-house of Hereford. Latin.
