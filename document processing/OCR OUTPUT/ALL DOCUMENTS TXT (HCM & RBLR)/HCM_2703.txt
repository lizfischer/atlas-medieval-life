HCM 2703
1299, Sunday, the day after the Feast of Ste Mark the Evangelist | April 26].
1. John le Seculer; 2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, by the hand of gominus William the chaplain, their receiver. QUITTANCE for half a mark in respect of fee for the term of the Annunciation. Given at Hereford. Latin.
