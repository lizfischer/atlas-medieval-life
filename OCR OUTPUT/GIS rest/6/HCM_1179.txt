 Annunciation of the Blessed wary [March 26]. 1. Roger de Malmeshull', vicar of the church of Preston’; 2. John Wyllies of Preston’,
 GRANT of one selion of land lying in the field called Prestonholme between the land of the said John on both sides and eee from the land of the dean and chapter of Hereford as far as the land of John de Ledelowe. Given at Preston’.
 Witnesses: Richard de creeenen ree Richard de Tyberton', Richard Piers, William Piers, John de la Pyrie.
