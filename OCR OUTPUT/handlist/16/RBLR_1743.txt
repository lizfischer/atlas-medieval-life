No. 2677. 1317, Friday, the day after the Feast of the Nativity of the Blessed Mary [September 9]. 1. Dean and chapter of Hereford, collectors of the procurations of Gaucelin and Luke de flisc', cardinals sent over to England by the Roman court; 2. The executors of Richard, late bishop of Hereford, by the hands of dominus Nicholas de Yanewrth', chaplain, their receiver. RECEIPT for eleven pounds sixteen shillings and four pence by reason of procurations due to the said cardinals from the property of the said bishop in the deanery and archdeaconry of Hereford for the first year of their stay in England. Given at Hereford. Latin.
