Oct. 4.—Walter,  portionist of Pontesbury,  promised  to pay  a fine 
                     of  one  hundred  shillings, when  required to do  so, for his 
                     repeated contumacy. 

                  WALTERUS    PORCIONARIUS  DE PontesBuRY.—Item    memorandum 
              est quod iiij Nonas Octobris, anno Domini  supradicto, concessit et 
              repromisit Walterus,  porcionarius ecclesie de Pontesbury, Domino 
              solvere, quandocumque  idem  Dominus  voluerit, C solidos pro mul- 
              tiplicibus contumaciis in  Ordinibus et  aliis, prout in retroactis 
              continetur.
