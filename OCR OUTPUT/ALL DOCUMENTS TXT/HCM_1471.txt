HCM 1471
1334, Friday next after the Feast of Saint
Mark the Evangelist (April 29]. 1. a2 pine Hugh de Clifford, perpetual vicar in the cathedral church of Hereford} 2. Thomas le Saltere of Hereford.
 GRANT, in fee-ferm, of on acre of lend in the suburb of Hereford Ss lying next to the land of the said Thomas, which acre and belongs to the vicarage.
 Rents twelve pence ras the first payment to be made on the feast of Saint Michael in the year 1335- Given at Hereford.
 Latin.
