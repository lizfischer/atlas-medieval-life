HCM 3005.E
1324, July 19.
LETTER from the abbot of wycemor' to the abbot of Dora end the prior of wormesleye excusing himself from sitting with them to hear the suit between the dean and chapter of Hereford and megister John de Ros, to which they had been appointed judges by the pope. Given in the monastery of Wyggemor',
 Latin. Sontemporary transcript.
