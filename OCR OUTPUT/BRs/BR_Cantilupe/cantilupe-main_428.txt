May  27.—Sir  Malcolm,  Canon  of Wells, was inducted to the Church 
                  of  Chetton on  the presentation of Hugh Burnell.  Letters of 
                  institution to be given at the next interview with the Bishop. 

                Matcotonvs.—vj    Kalendas  Junii, anno  Domini  M°CC°LXX° 
            nono, apud Ledebury, fuit dominus Malcolonus, Canonicus Wellensis, 
            inductus in  corporalem  possessionem ecclesie de Chetintone,  ad 
            presentacionem  domini  Hugonis  Burnel, militis, veri patroni; et 
            tunc dominus  decrevit quod  in proximo  colloquio suo adinvicem 
            idem  dominus  M[alcolonus]  institueretur litteratorie in ecclesiam 
            antedictam.
