No. 13. 1314. Saturday in the Vigil of Holy Trinity June 1. 1. Roger de Breynton' and William, his brother; 2. Magister Gilbert de Reygate, clerk. AGREEMENT for the annulment of the lease specified in No. 12 of even date subject to quiet possession for a term of six years of one croft of six acres of arable land in the vill of Chirchebreynton formerly held by Nicholas de la Calewe. Given at Hereford. Latin.
