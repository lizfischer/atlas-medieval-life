HCM 386
1305, Wednesday next after the Feast of the Finding of the Holy Cross [May 5].
1. William de Grandisono and Sybil, his wife;
2. Thomas de Heye.

GRANT, in consideration of homage and service of thirty shillings, of a tenement in the township of Oxenhale formerly held by Robert de Gorewall'.
Rent: six shillings and eight pence annually, suit of court at Oxenhale twice anually. Given at Oxenhale.
Witnesses: Nicholas de Stretedene, Ivellus de Hertlannde, Roger the marshal, John Crok, William Hathewy.
Latin. Seal.
