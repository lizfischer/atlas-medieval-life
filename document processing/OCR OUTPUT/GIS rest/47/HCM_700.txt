HCM 700
1311, Saturday the Feast of St. Dionysius [October 9]. 
1. William Noremon, citizen of Hereford; 2. Richard son of Richard Monyword, citizen of Hereford. GRANT of one acre of arable land lying in the field called Knyhtemorefeld' between the land of the dean and chapter of Hereford and the land of Walter son of William de la Hay and extending from the highway to Ruyshemore. Given at Hereford. Witnesses: Henry de Orleton', Roger Penk' and Phillip le Werrour, bailiffs of the king in Hereford, John Noremon, Walter son of Roger de Mora, Walter de Breynton', Roger de Ledebury, William le Herdemon. Latin.
