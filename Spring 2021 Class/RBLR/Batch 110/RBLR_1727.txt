No. 1006, 1317, Tuesday next after the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 17]. RECEIPT from John and Stephen de Wyk' to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for forty shillings sterling which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to them in his will. Latin. Seal.
