HCM 469
1305, April 13.
1, Walter de Euysham, chaplain; 
2. William de Mortuo Mari and Hugh de Breuse.

GRANT of lands and tenements at Mora Canonicorum had of the gift of William de Euysham, chaplain, Given at Hereford.

Witnesses: John Lythfot, John Pryde, Richard Thurgrym, John Monyword, Gilbert Blod, Henry de Lugwardyn, William Hamelyn, Nicholas Iwayn, Richard de Brochwardyn, Nicholas Wyoch, Roger Ocwelmen, John de la Barewe, Walter Tope.
Latin. Seal.
