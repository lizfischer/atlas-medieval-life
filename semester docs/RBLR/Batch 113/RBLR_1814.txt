No. 1033. 1318 (MS. 1317), Saturday the next after the Feast of Saint Benedict the abbot [March 25]. RECEIPT from Thomas de Retlyng' to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty shillings, being for one robe owing to him for the summer season after the death of said bishop. Given at Canterbury. Latin.
