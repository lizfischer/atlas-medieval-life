HCM 532
1302, Michaelmas | September 29.
  1. Agnes de Gutinga, daughter of John Andreu de Cloppeleye condany 2e Stephen de Cronebroc.
 AGREEMENT, in consideration of five shillings, for a lease for thirteen years of a house and curtilage in Brochamton' between the house of William ad fontem anda the house of Henry de la Hale.
 Witnesses: Robert Andreu de Cloppeleye, Nicholas le vreynss de brochemton', Nicholas le heyr of the same,
 William le heyward of the sali, Robert de Switinton', clerk.
 Latin.
