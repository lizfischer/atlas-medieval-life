HCM 1711
1330, 12 Kal. November October 21j.
. 
 LETTER from Gaucelin, bishop of Albano, to the bishop of Worcester, to absolve John de Thormerton', priest of the diocese of Worcester, from a sentence of excommunication passed against him, and from the offence of celebrating divine service whilst under the said sentence of excommunication. Given at Avignon.
 Latin.
