No, 1001, 1317, Thursday next after the Feast of Saint lark the Evangelist [April 28], RECEIPT from Thomas de Retlyng * to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford and one of the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swinefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for forty shillings in consideration of service rendered by the said Thomas de Retlyng1 to the said dominus Richard in the affairs of the bishop before the king's justices in eyre at Canterbury, and his expenses in those affairs at Canterbury, Given at Hereford, Latin , Two seals . both broken at the edges.
