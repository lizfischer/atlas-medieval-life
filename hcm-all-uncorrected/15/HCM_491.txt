HCM 491
1334, July 12.
. 
 ORDINANCE of Thomas, bishop of Hereford, concerning the provision to be made for the vicar of the church of Dorston',. The bishop has ee the seid church to the prior and convent of fford', and orders that the said prior and convent are to allow the vicar to enjoy possession of certain houses ee the rectory and burisl-ground, and also certein lands, tithes, and other dues. Given at Bosebury.
 Latin. Cony \Printed more fully in Begistrum Thome de Charlton, pp.67-8).
