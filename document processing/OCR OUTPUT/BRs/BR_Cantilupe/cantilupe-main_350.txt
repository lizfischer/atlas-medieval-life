                           that it had  been ascertained by inspection of the Rolls that

                           inserted in error for that of Philip, and Arneley for Harley, 
                           and that arrears of sixteen pounds of rent from the date of the 
                           grant should  not  be claimed from   the executors of Bishop 
                           Thomas.
