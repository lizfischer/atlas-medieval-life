HCM 2720
1307, Saturday next before the Feast of St. Thomas the Martyr [Dec. 23].
1. Reginald de Orleton' and John de Clere of co. Hereford; 2. Dean and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford.
BOND for the payment of four and a half loads of corn of the value of six shillings and eight pence each, and for one load of oats of the value of six shillings.
Given at Hereford. Latin.
