No. 1008. 1317, Wednesday next after the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 18]. RECEIPT from Nicholas de Aka, portionist in the church of Ledebury in the diocese of Hereford, to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for six shillings bequeathed by the said dominus Richard in his will to the use of William and Ralph, the two servants (garciones) of Nicholas. Given at Hereford. Latin.
