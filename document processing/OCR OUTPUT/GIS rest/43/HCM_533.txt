HCM 533
1342/3, Tuesday next after the Feast of
 
 “7a ee balazioatice af tas Vingtn Kary Lrewuidy 4)e 1. Adem de Oulithe de Bromesberwe; 2. George le Meleward'.
 GRANT of a parcel of land lying in the field called Netheroulithesfeld' in length between the lend of John attehacche and the common(?) called le Gorst' and in width between the land of the said George and the land of Walter atte Grene. Given at Bromesberwe.
 witnesses: ‘Thomas Absolon, John Aubrey, William de Cholnhed', Robert Dyk', John atte hacche.
 Latin.
