Mar.  28.—Memorandum     of licence to study abroad for three years 
                          to Roger de Ludlow, Rector of Onibury. 

                       Onesury.—Item     memorandum     quod   magister  Rogerus  de 
                   Lodele habuit Herefordie, y Kalendas Aprilis, licenciam studendi in 
                   partibus transmarinis per  triennium ab Annunciacione  Beate  Vir- 
                   ginis, anno predicto; qui Rogerus est Rector  ecclesie de Onebury.
