HCM 2877
1315/6, Friday next after the Feast of St. Matthias the Apostle [February 27].
INJUNCTION of the sub-dean of Hereford, sitting in judgment in the greater church of Hereford, to Thomas de Ayleston', citizen of Hereford, under pain of major excommunication, to discharge a debt of sixty-nine shillings and nine pence which he owes to Hugh de Monyton', another citizen of Hereford, as a result of a purchase of clothes.
Latin. Broken seal.
