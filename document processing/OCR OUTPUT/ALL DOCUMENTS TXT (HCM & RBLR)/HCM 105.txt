Ho. 105. [1277]
1.
Stephen de Brunhope;
2.
Henry Thorgrym.
AGREEMENT for the lease of a tenement in
Hereford in the street of Beauual between the
tenement formerly of Agnes de Lustone and the
tenement of John Thorgrjn, to be held for two
years from the Feast of the Annunciation of
the Blessed Virgin, 1277, upon payment of
fifteen shillings and of one half mark to
repair the same.
Witnesses: Hugh the clerk, John Thorgrim, John de Glouceatr', Hugh de Aula, Roger de Rrunhopet William the clerk.
Latin.
