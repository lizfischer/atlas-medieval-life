No, 1022. 1317/8, Thursday next after the Feast of Saint Benedict the abbot [March 23]. RECEIPT from John Osebarn and Wymark' his wife to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty shillings which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to Wymark' in his will. Given at Canterbury. Latin. Chipped seal.
