HCM 201
1315, October 11.
1. Robert de Garewy;
2. Cecilia, his daughter.
GRANT of a messuage in Hereford lying opposite the church of St. Nicholas between the land of David de Rotherwas and the land of dominus Walter Oghwelman, chaplain.
Witnesses: Gilbert Blod, William de Clehungre, Roger de Bristollia, John Peres, Roger de Ledebury, David
de Rotherwas, Walter Theubaud.
Latin.
