No. 1020. 1317, Monday next after the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 16]. RECEIPT from Richard de Tornenton' to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty shillings which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin.
