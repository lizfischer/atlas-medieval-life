Feb. 13.—Sequestration  was  released, at the instance of the Earl of 
                     Cornwall, on  a moiety of  the fruits of William de Monkton, 
                     Rector of Dorsington.  Notice sent to Richard de  Heyton. 

                  DorsinTone.—Memorandum       quod  Idibus Februari, apud   Sug- 
              was,  anno  predicto, relaxavit Dominus,   ad  instanciam   Comitis 
              Cornubie,  medietatem  fructuum  domini  Willelmi  de  Moneketone, 
              Rectoris ecclesie de  Dorsingtone.   Et  littera ‘illius relaxacionis 
              directa fuit magistro R.  de Heytone.
