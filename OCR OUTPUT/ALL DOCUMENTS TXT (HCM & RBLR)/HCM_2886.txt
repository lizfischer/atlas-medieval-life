 APPOINTMENT Ly the dean and chapter of the church of Hereford of Richard de Auste snd John Fyn to receive seisin of a Messuage, forty acres of arable lend, six acres of meadow and three shillings' worth of rent in the suburbs of Hereford, of which they were enfeoffed by magister Thomas de Chaundos, archdeacon of Hereford.
