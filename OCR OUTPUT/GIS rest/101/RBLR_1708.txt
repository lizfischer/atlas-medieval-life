b . 1045, 1517, April 30, RECEIPT from Richard de Boklond' to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford and one of the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for five marks in part payment of ten marks which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Hereford, Latin, seal.
