HCM 427
circa 1300
1. Walter le Brut and Nicola, his wife; 2e Benedict de Holtleye.
 GRANT in fee-farm, in consideration of three pounds, of six acres of lend of which three lie in the field called Pichencumbe and three lie in the field called Bueld; with an agreement in the event of molestation to the grantee 2 twenty shillings to the holy land, and one half
 . mark to the sheriff for the time being for making the distraint.
 Rent: one rose annually.
 Witnesses: Henry Ythenard, William Hind', William de Piddemore, Gilbert de strodford', Aden Spillman, Richard
 e Strodford, Henry Pigoz. Latin. vo seals.
