No. 1372. 1320, 2 Kal. August [July 31].
LETTER from Walter, archbishop of Canterbury, to dominus A[dam], bishop of Hereford, giving him a mandate to demand of the executors an account of the administration of the will of dominus Richard, late bishop of Hereford, to examine the account submitted, and to release or condemn the executors according to their merits. The archbishop further commissions dominus Adam to give to theexecutors a letter of quittance, absolving them from further responsibilities. Given at Lambeth. Latin.
