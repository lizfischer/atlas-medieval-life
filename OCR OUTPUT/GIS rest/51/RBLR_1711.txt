No. 2724. [1317], Tuesday the Feast of the Invention of the Holy Cross [May 3]. 1. Joanna Eseger de Ledebur', 2. John de Kernes', treasurer of Hereford, executor [of the will of Richard] de Swinefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, and his co-executors. QUITTANCE for a suit of clothes of azure colour for the summer season, lined with green sindon, comprising . . . . . a cope and mantle with two hoods and second-best (secundis) gloves. Given at Ledebur'. Latin. Mutilated.
