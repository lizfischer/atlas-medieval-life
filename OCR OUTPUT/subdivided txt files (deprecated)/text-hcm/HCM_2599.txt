HCM 2599
1312, 13 Kal. June [May 20].
1. Bishops of Lincoln and London, collectors in England, Scotland, Ireland, and Wales of the tenth lately imposed for three years by Pope Clement V for the Holy Land; 2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, subcollectors, by the hands of John de Barwe. QUITTANCE for fifty-nine pounds sixteen shillings and eight pence for the last term of the third year of the said period of three years to the use of Henry Nasard, king's merchant. Given at London. Latin.
