No. 1034, 1317* April 16. RECEIPT from the dean and chapter of the church of Saint Paul at London, [to the executors of the will] of Richard de Swinefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for a hundred shillings which the said Richard [bequeathed to them in his will] . Given in their chapter-house at London, Latin . Faded in parts.
