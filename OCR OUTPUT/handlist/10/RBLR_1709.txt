No. 1046. 1317, Saturday next after the Feast of Saint Mark the Evangelist [April 30]. RECEIPT from Katerina de Geynuyle, prioress of Acornebur', to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford and one of the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for a silver cup which the said dominus Richard had assigned to her during his lifetime, and which frater Geoffrey de Geynuyle, her father, had sent to him from Ireland. Given at Acornebur'. Latin.
