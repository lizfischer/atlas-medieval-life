 1. Thomas de Holeye and Philip de la More, clerks, subeollectors in the diocese of Hereford of the four years' tenth lately imposed on the clergy by Pope John XXII;
 2. Dean and chapter of the cathedral church of Hereford.
 RECEIPT for twenty-five pounds three shillings and eleven pence halfpenny in respect of the moiety of this tenth for the second term, yig. for the term of St. Martin in the second year of the eforeseid period of four years. Given at Hereford.
