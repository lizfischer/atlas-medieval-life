HCM 1059
1309/10, 4 Kalends February [January 29].
REQUEST from Richard, bishop of Hereford, to the dean and chapter of Hereford, to deliberate on and give a suitable reply to a letter he had received from dominus John, bishop of Llandaff, a copyof which letter accompanied Richardon's request to the dean and chapter. Given at Sugwas. Latin.
