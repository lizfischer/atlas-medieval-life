HCM 2717
1306/7, January 16.
1. Bishops of Lincoln and London, appointed collectors in the whole of England, Scotland, Ireland and Wales of the tenth lately imposed for two years by Pope Clement V for the Holy Land;
2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, subcollectors, by the hand of dominus Ranulf de Evesham, their clerk.
QUITTANCE for eighty pounds in respect of the sum of the said tenth for the second term of the first year. Given at St. Paul's, London. Latin.
