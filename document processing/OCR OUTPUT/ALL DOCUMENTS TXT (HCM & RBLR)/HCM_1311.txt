HCM 1311
1314, Wednesday, April 10.
1. William de Brewos', lord of the honour of Brember and Gower; 2. John Iweyn. GRANT of a licence to build a watermill with a mill-pond on John Iweyn's own land in the fee of Ostrem' and to divert water to the mill as seems expedient. Given at Ostrem'. Witnesses: domini Robert de Penres, Richard Hakelute, knights, Devyd de Ia Bere, Peter Knoyl, Elias de Roilly, Robert Knoyl, William son of Adam Knoyl. Latin. Seal.
