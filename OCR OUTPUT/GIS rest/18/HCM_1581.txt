HCM 1581
1306, Saturday, the morrow of the Feast of St. John the Baptist [June 25].
1. Frater John, abbot, and the convent of St. Peter's, Gloucester; 2. John Polgroyn. GRANT of a corrody in food and drink in the monastery of St. Peter, and the same for one groom; also a chamber for his use, and sustenance for his horse; also an annual pension of three and a half marks for his robe; also a robe for his groom. Given in the chapter-house at Gloucester. Latin. Seal.
