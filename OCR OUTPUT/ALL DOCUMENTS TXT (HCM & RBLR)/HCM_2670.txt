HCM 2670
1306, Saturday, the day after the Feast of St. Kalixtus [October 15].
1. Robert de Cotinton', clerk of the dean and chapter of Hereford;
2. The said dean and chapter by the hand of dominus W. the Young.
QUITTANCE for twenty shillings due by reason of his office for the term of St. Michael 1306, viz. for the preceding half year. Given at Hereford. Latin. Seal.
