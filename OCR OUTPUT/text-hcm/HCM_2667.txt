HCM 2667
1305, Saturday next after the Feast of St. Luke the Evangelist [Oct. 23]. 
1. John de Bromfeld; 2. Dean and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford, by the hands of John de Barwe. RECEIPT for one mark in respect of his fee for the term of St. Michael, 33 Edw. [I], and for the term of the Annunciation, 34 Edw. [I]. Given at London. Latin. Chipped Seal.
