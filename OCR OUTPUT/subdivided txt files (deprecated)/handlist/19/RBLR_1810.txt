No. 1047. 1317/8, Thursday next after the Feast of Saint Benedict the abbot [March 23]. RECEIPT from Simon de Scheluyng' to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for ten marks which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Canterbury. Latin.
