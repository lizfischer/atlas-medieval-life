No. 1030. 1317, Tuesday next after the Feast of Saint John ante portam latinam [May 10] . RECEIPT from Adam de Ailyneton', once the personal clerk of dominus Richard, formerly bishop of Hereford, to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford and one of the executors of the will of the said dominus Richard, for ten marks and four silver spoons which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin. Two seals, both imperfect.
