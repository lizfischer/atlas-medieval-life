1315/6, Saturday next before the Feast of St. Mary in Marcio [March 20].
1. Walter de Weston'; 2. John le leche de Walsned'.
GRANT, in consideration of eighteen shillings, of a messuage at le Mongoye between the land of Gilbertle Coliar' and the land of Walter de Cimiten' and extending from the highway to the land of the hospital of St. Katherine. Given at Ledbury.
