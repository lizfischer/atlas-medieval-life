June 1—The    Bishop commissions Gilbert de Middleton, Archdeacon 
                                 of Northampton,  to act for him in the matter of an exchange 
                                 of  a  prebendal portion of  Burford,  held by  William  de 
                                  Ayermin, with  a Canonry  of St. Patrick's, Dublin, held by 
                                  Walter de Islip.
