In a general  chapter of the Cisterctans tt was decided that the abbot 
                           of Dore  should obey  the bishop who  was  de  facto diocesan. 

                         Lecta  inquisicione super querela domini  Menevensis  episcopi in 
                    capitulo Cisterciensi, lectis eciam litteris superius in hoc  filo con- 
                    tentis, respondit  dominus   abbas  Cisterciensis in  ipso capitulo in 
                    audiencia  coram  cunctis quod   non  fuit capituli diffinire de limiti- 
                    bus diocesium,  et idem  decrevit ex consensu totius capituli generalis 
                    quod  abbas  Dorensis  obediret illi qui est in possessione loci abbacie 
                    jure diocesano   et non  alteri.
