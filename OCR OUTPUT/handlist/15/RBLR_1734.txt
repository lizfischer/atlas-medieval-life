No, 1044. 1317, Wednesday next after the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 18]. RECEIPT from William de Molendino de Bosebur' to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty shillings which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. This money William received from dominus Nicholas de Ak', portionist in the church of Ledebur'. Given at Hereford. Latin. Seal.
