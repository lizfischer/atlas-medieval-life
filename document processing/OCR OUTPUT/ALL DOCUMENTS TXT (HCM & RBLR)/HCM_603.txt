1336, Sunday next after the Feast of St.
Am se (April 7). 1. Matilda, relict of Emmorius son of Walter James de Walforde, widow; 2. Margery, daughter of Walter James.
 RELEASE of two acres of land and a pareel of meadow, : as specified in No,@00 dated Thursday next after the Feast of the Conception of the Virgin Mary, 1336. : Given at Walford’,
 Witnesses: Richard Adames, Adem le helyon, Henry de Warhulle, Robert Dun, Thomas de Blakeneye, William Tulle, Henry Tull’.
