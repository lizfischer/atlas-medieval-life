HCM 687
Early 14th century.
,  1. Henry le Masun de Lunhales; 2. John son of Vincent de Deweswalle.
 GRANT, in consideration of eight shillings, of one eere of land lying in the field called Aluerdeshull' between the land of Stephen de la Grene and the land of the said John son of Vincent and extending from the highway to the land of John de Turburuile.
 Witnesses: William Maniaunt, William de la Calewe, Hugh Helle, John Boter, Nicholas Atewelle.
 Latin.
