1315/6, the Feast of the Purification of the Virgin Mary [February 2].
1. John, dean, and chapter of Hereford;
2. Hugh son of William le Stedemon de Ros, clerk.
LEASE for three years as from the aforesaid date of the tithes with houses, buildings, etc., of the chureh of Oppton Episcopi.
Rent: twenty-five marks annually.
Sureties: William le Webbe de Ros, Thomas le Webbe and Nicholas le Webbe, sons of the said William.
Latin.
