HCM 2251
1314, September 26,
LETTERS PATENT of Edward II grenting a licence for the alienation in mortmain by magister Robert de Gloucestre to the dean and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert, Hereford, of a rent of twenty shillings in Hereford for an obit for him every year in their church. By fine of twenty shillings.
Latin. [Printed in W. W. Capes, Charters and Records of Hereford Cathedral, p.182].
