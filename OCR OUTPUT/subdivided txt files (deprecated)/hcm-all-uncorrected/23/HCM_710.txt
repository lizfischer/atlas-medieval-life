1350, Thursday next efter the Feast of
the Nativity of our Lord {December 30]. 1, John Mon ani Alice his wife, Peter le Fourches, Isabel his wife, and David le Parkeres 2. Thomas Gibbes and Matilda his wife,
 GRANT of lands and tenements in the suburb of Hereford lately belonging to John Thrum, Given at Hereford,
 Witnesses: John le Baylif, bailiff of the king in
 ‘ the city of Hereford, John Shekelok’, John de la Berne, ‘ Walter Pigage, Rozer de Rellmar’, datin.a ee Oe nN a ee ee ee ee ee 963.
