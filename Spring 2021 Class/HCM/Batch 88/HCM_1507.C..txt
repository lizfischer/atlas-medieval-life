HCM 1507.C.
1316, 3 Non. May [May 5].

 MANDATE of Richard [Swinfield], bishop of Hereford, to his official to excommunicate those citizens who had usurped coercive powers in the bishop's fee, in defiance of an agreement made between Bishop Peter d'Aigueblanche and the citizens of Hereford. Given at Bosebury.
Latin. Transcript dated circa 1400. [Printed in Registrum Ricardi de Swinfield, pp. 510-11].
