1312/3, Saturday, the Feast of SS. Fabian and Sebastian [Jan. 20].
1. J[ohn], dean, and the chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford; 2. Dominus William Vallet, subtreasurer. QUITTANCE for certain vestments, described in detail, which have been sent to the church of Dudelebur'. Given in the chapter-house at Hereford. Latin.
