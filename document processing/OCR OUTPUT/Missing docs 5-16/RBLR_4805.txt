RBLR 4805 
1348.4.13

Hatfield, 1348.04.13 [ides of April]—Bishop [Thomas de Lisle Bishop of Ely] receives certification of commission for exchange [enacted on 1348.4.7 by Wolstan de Bransford Bishop of Worcester] between Roger de Breynton (now Rector of Doddington) and Richard Berde de Ledbury (now Archdeacon of Gloucester)]; Roger de Breynton swears canonical obedience “apparently in person” (per RMH)
