                            pounds, in  payment  of Peter's pence for the two years 1828 
                            and  1329.

                     domino,  etc., tenebatur racione denariorum  beati Petri per  ipsum 
                     de  episcopatu   Herefordensi  domino   nostro, etc., debitorum   de
