No. 1037. 1317, Tuesday the Feast of the Invention of the Holy Cross [May 3]. RECEIPT from Thomas de Marisco de Bos' to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford and one of the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for one closed (clausa) surcoat, red in colour and lined (forrata) with satin, with one hood lined with fur, both of which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Bosebur'. Latin.
