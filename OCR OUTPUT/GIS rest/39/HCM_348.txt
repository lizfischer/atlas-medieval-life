HCM 348
1331/2, Thursday next after Epiphany | January 9.
,  1. Richard Dastyn of Grete near Wynchecombe; 2. John de Schokereswelle of Clifford’ Chaumbrer on the Stour.
 BOND for the payment of sn annual rent of twenty pounds in respect of lands in Clifford’. Given at Clifford’.
 Witnesses: Walter Schokereswelle, Peter de wynecote, Thomas Beaumount', William de Cliue of Chaueringworthe.
 Latin.
