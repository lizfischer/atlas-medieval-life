No. 1009. 1317, Saturday next after the Feast of Saint Mark the evangelist [April 30], RECEIPT from Walter de Dunr' and Richard de Boklonde to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swinefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for all the letters, documents, records, and other writings concerning their lands and buildings in Wymelyngewelde and Estenor' . Given at Hereford. Latin .
