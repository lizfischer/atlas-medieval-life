HCM 1902
1321, Thursday next after the Feast of the
Annunciation of the Virgin Mary LMarch 26].
 1. Adam son of Benet le Hopere de Ledeburi;
 2. Richard Berde de Ledeburi and Alina his wife. j
 GRANT of ali his arable land with its appurtenances in Ledeburi forayne and Estenovere. Given at Ledbury.
 Witnesses: Walter de Westone, William le Broy, John Shorbarn, Ralph le Potiler, Henry le Skynnar'.
 Erench.
