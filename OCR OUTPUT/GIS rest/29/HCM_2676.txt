HCM 2676
1315/6, Wednesday next after the Feast of St. Valentine [February 18].
1. Dean and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford;
2. Prior of Lanthony Prima in Wales, by the hands of John de Barewe, seneschal of the said dean and chapter. RECEIPT for seven marks in part payment of a bond in the sum of ten pounds. Given in the chapter-house at Hereford. Latin. Fragmentary seal.
