Feb.  17.—Commission   to  the bishop of St.  Asaph  to reconctle the 
                          church  and cemetery of Clun,  which  had  been desecrated by 
                          the murder  of a fugitive dragged from sanctuary, and also to 
                          consecrate altars and confirm children.
