Late 13th. century!
1. Cicely, widow of Adam de Welynton*; 2. Henry Edrich, citizen of Hereford.

 Witnesses: William de Haya, Bartholomew Lespicer, Walter Thebaud, Adam Spart, Adam Pot, Thomas Helle, John le Taillur, William de Lecche, clerk.
