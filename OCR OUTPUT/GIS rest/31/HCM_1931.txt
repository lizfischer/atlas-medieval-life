HCM 1931
1315, 12 Kal. June [May 21].
LETTER of Richard [Swinfield], bishop of Hereford, to the dean and chapter of the cathedral church of Hereford, quoting a letter which he has received from King Edward [II].
The royal letter, dated 14 May 1315, reproves the bishop for his tardiness in paying the tenth granted to the king by the Pope, and urges him to do so with all haste, especially in view of the bad news received from Berewyk’ sur Twede, The royel letter is given at Thonderlee, The bishop's letter is given at Bosebur’.
Latin and French. Stained.
