HCM 2157
1335, July 8.
. 
 PROHIBITION from King Edward LIII] to the dean and chapter of Hereford, persons of the church of Dudelebury, for-bidding them to proceed in the Court Christian with the suit against Robert de Tene, clerk of the chapel of Mersehton', touching two parts of the said chapel which the dean end chapter claim until the matter is discussed in the king's court. Tested at Carlisle.
 Latin.
