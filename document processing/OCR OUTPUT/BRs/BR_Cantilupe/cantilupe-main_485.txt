Mar. 7.—Dispensation  on account of illegitimacy granted to Reginald 
                   de  Radnor, and  letters dimissory for all Holy Orders to be 
                   conferred by  the Bishop of Llandaff. 
                 DIsPENSACIO  ET DIMISSORIE  REGINALDI  DE RADENORE,  8COLARIS. 
             —Memorandum      quod,  eisdem  die et  loco et anno,  dispensavit 
             Dominus  cum   Reginaldo de Radenore,  scolari, in forma communi, 
             secundum  tenorem  rescripti Apostolici super defectu natalium quem 
             patitur de subdiacono genitus et soluta.  Insuper eidem Reginaldo 
             Dominus,  per suas litteras dimissorias, concessit quod a Venerabili 
             Patre, Domino   W([illelmo de Bruce], Dei  gracia Landavensi  Epi- 
             scopo, possit ad omnes Ordines  minores et sacros licite promoveri; . 
             dum  tamen  canonicum, ete.
