HCM 90
1317, Tuesday the morrow of St. Mark the Evangelist [April 26]. 
1. Alice formerly wife of John Lightfot, formerly citizen of Hereford, deceased; 2. Hugh de Monyton', citizen of Hereford. RELEASE of a messuage in a street called Sadelwruyhtestret' in the city of Hereford, in the occupation of the said Hugh and lately purchased by him of the executors of the will of the said John. Given at Hereford. Witnesses: Gilbert Blod, Thomas de la Barre, Richard de Cruce, Thomas Thope, Thomas Hamound, William Russel, Roger de Bristoll'. Latin.
