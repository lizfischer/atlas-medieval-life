Dec.  18.—The  Bishop, by direction of the King, writes to the Dean 
                         and  Chapter that they receive to the Prebend of Withington 
                         Hugh   de Leominster, who  was  duly presented by the King 
                         during  the vacancy of the See.

                   cepimus  tenorem qui sequitur continentes: Edwardus, etc., venerabili

                   spectantem,  racione episcopatus predicti tunc vacantis et in manu 
                   nostra existentis, habendam cum  suis juribus et pertinenciis quibus-

                   mandassemus   dilectis nobis in Christo, Decano et Capitulo ecclesie
