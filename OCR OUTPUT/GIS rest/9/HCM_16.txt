HCM 16
Lgirea 1300.
, 
 1. John Broun' de Breynton’ 2. Roger son of Walter, medians of Hereford.
 GRANT of two selions of arable land lying in the field of Breynton’ lying at Huwescrose between the land of John de la berne and the land of William the shepherd and extending to the wey called Huwescrose.
 Witnesses: John de Seuenok', Hugh le Shypward, Adam Wyty, William Broher, Adam son of Adam Wyty.
 Latin. Seal.
