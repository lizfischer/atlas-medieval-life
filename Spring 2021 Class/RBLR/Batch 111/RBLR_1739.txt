No. 1036. 1317, the day before the Ides of July [July 14]. RECEIPT from the tutors (creditores) of the Franciscan friars at Canterbury to magister Richard Hamenherse, an executor of the will of the late dominus Richard, formerly bishop of Hereford, for forty shillings, handed to them by the said magister Richard. Given at Canterbury. Latin. Seal broken at the edges.
