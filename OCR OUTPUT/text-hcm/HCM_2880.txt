HCM 2880
1317, May 26.
ROYAL MANDATE to the dean and chapter of the church of Hereford to cause Thomas de Penebrugg' who has been granted the treasurership and the prebend of Berton’ in the aforesaid church, to have the houses in Hereford which belonged to John de Kemeseye, late treasurer and prebendary of Berton’ aforesaid. Westminster. Latin.
