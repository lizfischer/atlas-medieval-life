HCM 2721
1309, December 3.
1. Bishops of Lincoln and London, appointed collectors in the whole of England, Scotland, Ireland and Wales of the tenth lately imposed for five years by Pope Clement V for the Holy Land; 2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, sub-collectors, by the hand of John de Wytteneye, their clerk. QUITTANCE for one hundred and thirty-two pounds in respect of the sum of the said tenth collected by them for the first term of the third year of the said period of five years. Given at London. Latin.
