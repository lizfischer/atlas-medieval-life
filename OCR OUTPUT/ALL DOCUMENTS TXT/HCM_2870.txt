HCM 2870
1306, Tuesday next after the Feast of St. Lucia the Virgin [Dec. 20]. 
1. Richard de Hamenays, sub-dean of Hereford and proctor of dominus John de Aqua Blanca, dean of Hereford; 2. Dominus Willam the Young, chaplain, receiver of the chapter of Hereford.
QUITTANCE for certain allowances (specified) payable to the dean in respect of the farm of Lydeneye, small commons and Peter's pence. Given at Hereford. Latin.
