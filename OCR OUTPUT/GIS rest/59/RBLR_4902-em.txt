No. 2690. 1349, June 8. INSPEXIMUS by the dean and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford of a charter given at Hereford, June 7, 1349, whereby dominus John de Trillek', bishop of Hereford, released to John de Gayton' two shillings of an annual rent of forty-two shillings and nine pence payable in respect of lands and tenements in the vill of Nethershelewyk' in allowance for a ditch in the aforesaid vill called Walneyeslake which the said John released to the bishop. Given at Hereford. Witnesses: magister John Rees, magister John de Oo, Roger de Breynton', magister William de Fowehope, magister John Profit. The inspeximus is given in the chapter-house at Hereford. Latin.
