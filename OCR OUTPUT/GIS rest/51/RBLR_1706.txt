Ho. 1025. 1317, Saturday next after the Peast of Saint Mark the Evangelist [April 30]. RECEIPT from Walter de Dunr' to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for all the letters, documents, records, and other writings concerning all his lands and buildings In Bromyard both within and without, which had been in the custody of the said dominus Richard , Given at Hereford, Latin. Endorsed in a contemporary hand ..... Bromyard Hoddesbach'
