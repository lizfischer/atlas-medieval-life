HCM 2598
1311/2, Jan. 27.
1. Bishops of Lincoln and London, collectors in England, Scotland, Ireland, and Wales of the tenth lately imposed by Pope Clement V for three years for the Holy Land; 2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, subcollectors, by the hands of John Barwe, their proctor. QUITTANCE for fifty pounds in respect of the said tenth. Given at London. Latin.
