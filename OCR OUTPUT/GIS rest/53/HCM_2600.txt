HCM 2600
1313, 7 Kal. July [June 25].
1. Bishops of London and Lincoln, collectors in England Scotlend, Ireland, and Wales of the tenth lately imposed for one year by Pope Clement V for the Holy Lands; 2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, sub-collectors, by the hands of dominus John de Whyteneie, their clerk and proctor. QUITTANCE for one hundred and forty pounds for the first term of the said one year tenth. Given at London. Latin.
