No. 1019. 1317, the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 12]. RECEIPT from John de Whiteneye, vicar in the church of Hereford, successor of dominus Walter de Banastre, formerly chaplain in the aforesaid church celebrating divine service for the soul of dominus William le Rous, to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud formerly bishop of Hereford, for one precious missal according to the usage of Hereford, at the end of which were written the meditations of the blessed Ancelmus; this missal the bishop himself had received from the said dominus Walter as a loan. Given at Hereford. Latin. Two seals, one chipped.
