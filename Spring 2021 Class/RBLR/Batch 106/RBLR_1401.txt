1314.6.1
No. 12. 1314, Saturday in the Vigil of Holy Trinity June 1. 1. Gilbert de Reygate, clerk; 2. Domini Roger de Breynton' and William his brother. LEASE for six years of one messuage and arable land in the vill of Chirehebreynton'. Rent; eight shillings annually. Given at Hereford. Latin.
