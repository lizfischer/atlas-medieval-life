July  11.—Mandate    of the  Court of  Canterbury  summoning   the 
                                  Bishop and  the  Official to answer the appeal of  William 
                                  de Irby, Prior of St. Guthlac’s, from the sentence of excom- 
                                  munication pronounced  against  him  for administering  the 
                                  sacraments of the Church to one who was not his parishioner.
