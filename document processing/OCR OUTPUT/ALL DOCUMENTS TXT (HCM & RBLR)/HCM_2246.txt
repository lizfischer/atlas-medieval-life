HCM 2246
1312, December 28.
INSPEXIMUS by Edward II of a charter dated 1 March 1241 inspecting and confirming a charter dated at La Suze 7 September 1202 [see Calender of Charter Rolls, Vol. I, p. 256] in favour of the canons of Hereford, confirmation of the same, with a further grant of liberties and privileges [for which see ibid, Vol. 3, pp. 212-3]. At the bottom is a copy of a notification dated 1253 by Anselm, dean of Hereford, that the citizens of Hereford bestowed fifteen marks on the church of Hereford, in return for which gift they shall be free of aids, tallages, customs and exactions. Latin. Copy.
