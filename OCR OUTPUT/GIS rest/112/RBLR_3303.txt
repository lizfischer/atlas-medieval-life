1333.8.29
943 29 August 1333 Hereford. Roger de Breynton, archdeacon of Worcester, canon of Hereford and vicar general of Adam [Orleton], bishop of Worcester, he being in remote parts, to Br. Nicholas de Stanlak, monk of Worcester, appointing him warden (custos) of the feretories of St. Oswald and St. Wulstan, vacant by the death of Philip de Pyribrok. This he wishes to be observed (volumus esse notam) by the prior and chapter of Worcester and all others concerned.
