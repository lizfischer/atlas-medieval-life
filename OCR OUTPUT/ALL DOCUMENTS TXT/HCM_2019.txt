Late 13th. ceatury
. BOND from John de Gloucestr', citizen of Hereford, to magister William de Radenor' to allow the latter to distrain for arrears of the rent of twelve shillings issuing annually from the tenement of the almshouse of Hereford which he sold to magister Willian.
 Witsesses: John de la hulle, John le Waunter, Hugh Elote (?), bailiffs of the king in Hereford, Walter de la Barre, John Lytfot, Bartholomew le spicer, Richard de Bramford, Roger de Bruneshope, Adam the painter, Hugh de Maddel', John son of Thomas Russel, Nicholas Wynter, Richard de Brocwardin', William de Leache, clerk,
 Latin. Seal.553.
