Sept. 27.—Letter  to Maurice  de Membury   bidding  him  to act on 
                   instructions from John  de  Clare, who is sent to England to 
                   audit the accounts and on  other business. 

                Maovricio pz Memsury.—Item    tune  Dominus  seripsit statum 
            suum  Mauricio de Membury,  et quod dominum   Johannem  de Clare 
            in Angliam misit ad compotos  audiendos et alia negocia facienda ; 
            et quod  eidem J(ohanni]  crederet in hiis que ei diceret ex parte 
            sua.  Datum  ut supra.
