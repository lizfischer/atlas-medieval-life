Sept. 9—The    Bishop  of Worcester allows the Church  of  Coln  St. 
                                    Denys, which  had been polluted by bloodshed, to be reconciled 
                                    by any  Catholic Bishop.   The Bishop  of Hereford, therefore, 
                                    performs the ceremony  on Feb. 2  following.
