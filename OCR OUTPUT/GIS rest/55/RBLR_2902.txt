105 17 October 1329 Blockley. Memorandum that the bishop collated the church of Hanbury-by-Droitwich (Hambur' iuxta Wych) to Roger de Breynton, priest, and instituted him (et ipsum canonice instituit in eadem). It was vacant by the death of M. John de Renham. He ordered the sacrist of Worcester to induct.
