RBLR 1303
1313.10.23
No. 17. 1313, Tuesday next before the feast of St. Luke the Evangelist October 23. 1. John Broun de Breynton'; 2. Roger le Leche de Hereford'. GRANT of one and a half acres of land lying in Oldewallesfeld' between the land of Roger de Breynton' and the land of Richard Bryan and extending from the land of the said Roger de Breynton' as far as the highway. Given at Breynton'. Witnesses: John de Seuenak', Roger de Breynton', John de la Berne, Adam Whyty, Bartholomew the clerk. Latin. Seal.
