Jan. 15.—Certificate of the Official of the Archdeacon of Northampton 
                                     that an  inquiry proves the Church  of Broughton,  valued at 
                                     11 marks,  to be vacant by the death of Simon  de Addington, 
                                     the last Rector ; and that it is in the patronage of the Abbess 
                                     and Convent  of St. Mary  des Prés.
