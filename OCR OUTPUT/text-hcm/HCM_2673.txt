HCM 2673
1308, July 12.
1. Gilbert de Bruera, clerk; 2. Dean and chapter of the church of Hereford by the hands of John de Barwe. QUITTANCE for sixty shillings as part of the salary due to him for his services as notary in the inquiry concerning the miracles of Thomas de Cantilupe, one time bishop of Hereford. Given at London. Latin.
