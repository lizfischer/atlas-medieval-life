HCM 1439
1308, July 11.
 RECEIPT of Guillelmus Testa, archdeacon of Aran in the church of Comminges, chaplain and envoy of the pope, for twenty pounds received of the dean and chapter of Hereford by the hand of John de Barewe in payment of his expenses in the suit for the canonisation of dominus Thomas de Cantilupo, formerly bishop of Hereford. Given at London.
 Latin.
