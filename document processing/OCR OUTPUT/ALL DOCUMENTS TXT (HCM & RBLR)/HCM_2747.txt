HCM 2747
1301, the day after the Feast of St.
Leodegar LOcte 3]. 1. William Valet, chaplain, subtreasurer of the church of Hereford; 2 Deen and chapter of the aforesaid church, by the hand of
 Sominus william called the Young.
 QUITTANCE for twenty-four shillings for the purchase of oil for the term of the Annunciation, and for the term of St. Michael following. Given at Hereford.
 Latin.
