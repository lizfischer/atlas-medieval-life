HCM 1271
Late 13th century
Cecilia, latewife of Walter Syward' of Hereford, widow; 2. Dean and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford.
 RELEASE of three acres of land and of a third part of all the land formerly in the occupation of the said Walter in Wyneston' and elsewhere in the suburbs of Hereford.
 Witnesses: John Lictfot and Bartholomew Lespecer, bailiffs of the said dean and chapter, William de Welinton', Roger de Gardino, Henry de la Neuton', John Alekin, Adam le Pichere, Adam de Barewe.
 Latin.536.
