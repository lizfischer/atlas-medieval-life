Jan.  17.—The   Bishop  issues to  Hugh,  Vicar of  Lydney,  Letters 
                          patent  exempting   him from   molestation in  the exercise of 
                           his ministry, though he has lost Letters of Orders.  Similar 
                           letters are granted to the Vicar of Tidenham.
