HCM 2607
1330, Sept. 26,
. 
 1. Thomas de Boleye and Philip de la More, clerks, subcollectors appointed by Thomas, bi shop of Hereford, principal collector in his diocese of the four years' tenth lately imposed by Pope John XXII;
 2e Dean and chapter of the cathedral church of Hereford.
 RECEIPT for twenty-five pounds three shillings and eleven pence halfpenny in respect of the first payment of the said tenth. Given at Hereford.
 Latin.
