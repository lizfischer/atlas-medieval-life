No. 1192. 1317, Monday the Feast of St. Nicholas, Confessor [December 6].

1. Henry de la Hale of Seggesbarwe, chaplain;
2. Stephen de Seggesberwe.

GRANT of lands and tenements held of the grant of the said Stephen in the vill of Aylnsston near Stour. Given at Aylnaston.
Witnesses: John de Hayford, John Franceis of Etyndon, Hugh le Freman, WIlliam Parys, William de Wylicote, William de Quenton.
