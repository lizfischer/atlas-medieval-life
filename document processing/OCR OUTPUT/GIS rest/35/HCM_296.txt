HCM 296
Late 13th. ~ early 14th. century
1. John de Stanuord', by assent of Henry, his eldest son; 2. Thomas de Stanford, his son.
 GRANT in fee-farm of half a virgate of land in Netherida which Gilbert, formerly the reeve, held ol grantor.
 Rent: six pence annually.
 Witnesses: Adam, chaplain of Bromhard,
 Peter de bosco, William de Halh'd, Richard de hida, Henry de hida. Latin.| 555.
