HCM 2879
1317, Tuesday next after the Feast of Easter [April 5].
1. John de Sevenak'; 2. Dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer in the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford. RELEASE of five acres of arable land in Chyrchebroynton', of which John Toky enfeoffed magister Roger de Sevenak', formerly canon of Hereford, the grantor and Roger de Sevenak', the grantor's brother. Given at Hereford. Witnesses: William de Stretton', John de Brugge, Gilbert Ives, Hugh le Schypward, William de Byspewalle, Adam Hwyty, Henry de Underwode. Latin.
