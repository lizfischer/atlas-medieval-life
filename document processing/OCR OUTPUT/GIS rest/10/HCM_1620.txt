 1. Thomas, earl of Lancaster and Leicester, seneschal of bael and
 2. Abbot of Gloucester.
 REQUEST for the loan of one wagon or five horses to help carry his victuals on the campaign which the king of England intends to wage against his enemies abroad Mees The earl wishes the wagon or horses to be ready at his castle of Pontefract on the Feast of the Trinity next following.
