HCM 621
1333, Wednesday next after the Feast of
St. Clemens (November 24]. 1. Gilbert de ee 2. dosn de Rodebergh', lady of Nategraue.
 GRANT of the manor of La Leye with its tenements and tenants in Stauerton', Heydon', Hawe, and Trilleye. Given at la Leye.
 Witnesses: Thomas son of Miles de Rodebergh', knight John Spilemon, Robert de eer: Thomes Pricke, Ralph de Walton', John Derneford', william de wyhtfeld'.
 Latin.
