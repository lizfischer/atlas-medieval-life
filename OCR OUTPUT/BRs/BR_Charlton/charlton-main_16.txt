April 5.—Commission   to William de Fownehope,  canon, to act with 
                          Richard  de Sidenhale in the suit between Thomas  de  Bolete, 
                          who  claims the treasurership of the cathedral, and John  de 
                          la  Chaumbre, who  is actually in possession of the dignity. -
