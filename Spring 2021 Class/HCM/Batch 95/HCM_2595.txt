HCM 2595
1309/10, Tuesday next before the Feast of St. Edward, king and martyr [March 17].
LETTER OF PROXY of Willism de Bliburg', canon in the church of Hereford, to magister John de Swynefeld, his confrere and precentor of the aforesaid church, to discuss and order with the dean and the other canons in matters affecting the said church. Given at London. Latin.
