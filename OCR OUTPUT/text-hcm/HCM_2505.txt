HCM 2505
1308, Monday, the Feast of St. Catherine the Virgin [November 25].
DEFEASANCE of a bond of statute merchant in twenty pounds between Walter Morewy of Nortone and the chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford in the absence of the dean, namely that the aforesaid Walter shall observe his covenant respecting two windmills in the vill of Nortone and pay thirty shillings annually. Given in the chapter-house at Hereford. Latin.
