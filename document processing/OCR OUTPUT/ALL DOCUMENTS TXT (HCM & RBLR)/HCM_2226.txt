HCM 2226
1308, Monday the morrow of the Feast of the Holy Trinity [June 10].
ORDER given at Chirebury in the suit between magister Stephen, rector of the church of Dunre, plaintiff, by
John Germeyn his proctor, and Robert de Dunre and Adam son
 of Williem of the same, suspending the latter from entering
 the church and fixing a place and day for hearing the suit.
Latin. Fragment.
