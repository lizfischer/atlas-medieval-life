HCM 181
1310, Monday next before the Feast of All Saints [October 26].
1. J[ohn], dean, and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford; 
2. James de Henle, rector of the church of Ros.
LEASE for life of a messuage in the city of Hereford lying near the tenement formerly belonging to Walter de la Borcote below the gate called Westyate and which Richard de Bere formerly held.
Rent: eleven shillings annually. Given in the chapter-house at Hereford.
Latin.
