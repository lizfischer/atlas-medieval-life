HCM 1658
1315/6, January 18.
1. Aymer de Valence, Earl of Pembroke, Lord of Wersefordia and Montignac; 2. John, abbot, and the convent of St. Peter's, Gloucester.
QUITCLAIM of the right to repair, to have the use of, and to fish in the weir (gurges) of Freinelode; also of all his right in the said weir and fishery with their appurtenances and the adjoining land called Tolyscroft'. Given at London.
Latin.
