HCM 1416
1285, 2 Kal. May [30 April].
COMMISSION of J[ohn], archbishop of Canterbury and primate of all England, directed to the Abbot of Oseneye, to the warden of the Franciscan Friars at Oxford, and to magister Herveus de Saham, professor of canonical law, appointing them to audit the accounts of the executors of the will of dominus Thomas, formerly bishop of Hereford, and to discharge the executors from responsibility for their administration. If all the above auditors cannot execute their duties together, then two of them are to carry them out; they must then inform the archbishop of their actions. Given at Mortelake. Latin.
