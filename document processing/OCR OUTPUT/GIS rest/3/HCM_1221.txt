HCM 1221
1336, Friday next after the Feast of St.
Mark Evangelist | April 26].
 1. Thomas de Lulham de eS es
 2. Dominus Nicholas Pere de Preston, chaplain.
 GRANT of a curtilage in the vill of Clehungur, lying between the messuage of Alice: Reinald' and the messuage of John de Stoney and extending from the highway through the middle of the said vill as far as the land of the canons of Hereford. Given at Clehungur.
 Witnesses: John de la Ree, Richard Gilbert, Adam Gibbons, John le yonge, Walter the brewer (brasour').
 Latin. Seal.
