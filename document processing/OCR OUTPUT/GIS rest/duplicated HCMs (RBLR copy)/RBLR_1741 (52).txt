No. 1035. 1317, August 2. RECEIPT from William de Suanton', rector of the church of Cherring' to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for one gold ring with a sapphire and an emerald, which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at London. Latin. Seal broken at the edges.
