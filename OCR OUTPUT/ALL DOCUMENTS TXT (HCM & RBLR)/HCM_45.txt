HCM 45
1316, Sunday nex after the Feast of St. Bartholomew the Apostle [August 29].
1. Roger de la Hulle de Hereford'; 2. Hugh de Monyton', citizen of Hereford. RELEASE of a messuage with houses and buildings which the aforesaid Hugh has by the grant of the executors of the will of John Lightfot, formerly citizen of Hereford. Given at Hereford. Witnesses: Richard de la Hay, bailiff of the dean and chapter of Hereford, Gilbert Blod, Richard Thurgrin, Thomas Thope, Roger le Tayllour, John Steuenes, Roger de Ledebury. Latin.
