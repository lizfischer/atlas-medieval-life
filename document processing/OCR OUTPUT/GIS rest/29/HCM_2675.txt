HCM 2675
1311, in the Feast of St. Praxedes, Virgin [July 21].
1. Henry de Henor', son and executor of the will of William de Henor', deceased; 
2. Dean and chapter of Hereford.
QUITTANCE for twenty shillings being the pension due to the aforesaid William for the year 1310 and 1311. Given at Hereford.
