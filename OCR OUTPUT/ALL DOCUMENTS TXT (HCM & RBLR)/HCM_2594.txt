HCM 2594
1309, Tuesday next after the Octave of Easter [April 8].
1. Giles, clerk, called the tailor (cissor); 2. Dean and chapter of the church of Hereford. RECEIPT for five marks in part payment of ten marks annual pension in which they were bound to him for his service of the year 1309, viz. for the term of Easter of the same year. Given St Hereford. Latin.
