July 6.—Memorandum   of a debt of £102  6s. 6d. to William Daubeny 
                  and  John  de Clare, executors of Hugh  de  Cantilupe, Arch- 
                  deacon  of Gloucester, to be repaid at Christmas, 1280; also 
                  of a  debt of one hundred  marks  to Peter of  Chester to be 
                  repaid at  Boston fair on July  8, 1280.   The copies of the 
                  acknowledgments  are strung on a file attached to the Register.
