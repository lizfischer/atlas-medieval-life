No. 2872. 1310/11, Tuesday next after the Feast of Epiphany [Jan. 12]. INDULGENCE of forty days granted by frater Gilbert, bishop of Annaghdown (Enachdunensis), to those who pray for the soul of dominus Nicholas de Reygate, late treasurer in the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford, whose body lies burled in the said church. Given at Hereford. Latin. Stained.
