RBLR 1707
1317.4.30
No. 1043, 1317* Saturday next after the Feast of Saint Mark the Evangelist (April 30), from Roger de Gattesden' to the executors of the will of Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for one bay horse cum curvo dorso called Bayard de Dor', of value a hundred shillings, which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will, Given at Hereford. Latin.
