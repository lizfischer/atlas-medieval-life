HCM 680
1310, Sunday next after the Feast of St. Giles the Abbot [September 6]. 
1, Stephen son of William Payn; 
2. John Iweyn.
GRANT of seven accres of arable land which formerly the grantor bought of Stephenson of John de Wormetone in the vill of Deweswalle, Given at Deweswalle. Witnesses: William de Calewe, Stephen de Grene, John de Grene, Nicholas de La Walle, John Boter, John Vyncent. Latin.
