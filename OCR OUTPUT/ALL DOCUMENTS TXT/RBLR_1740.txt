No. 1018. 1317, 16 Kalends of August [July 17]. RECEIPT from Thomas de la Felde, rightful proctor of Margery de Brok', daughter of Isabel de la Dane, formerly sister of the late dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, to the executors of the will of the said dominus Richard for ten marks which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to Margery in his will. Given at Canterbury. Latin.
