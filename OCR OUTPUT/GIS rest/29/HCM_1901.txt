HCM 1901
LFirst helf 14th century, June 10.
. 
 REQUEST of Bartholomew de Badelesuere to the dean and chapter of Hereford to pay to Gerum Pepe, Nicholas the falconer, and Henry the falconer, his duly appointed attorneys, the arrears of the three hundred pounds which the king assigned to him to receive of them of the moneys issuing from the tenth for six years according to the mandate of the king to the bishop of Hereford, Given at London.
