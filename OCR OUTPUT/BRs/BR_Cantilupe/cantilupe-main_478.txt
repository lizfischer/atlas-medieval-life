Jan, 24.—Licence   of non-residence to study for one year granted to 
                   Robert  de Lacy, Rector  of Brampton  Brian.   Release of the 
                   sequestration for non-residence and for non-appearance at the 
                   Ordination.

            per unum annum   continuum a festo Sancti Michaelis, anno Domini
