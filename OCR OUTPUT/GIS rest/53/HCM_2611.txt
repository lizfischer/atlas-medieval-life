HCM 2611
1333, November 10.
. 
 1. Walter Cerles and Philip de la More, clerks, subcollectors in the diocese of Hereford of the four yesrs' tenth lately imposed on the clergy by Pope John XXII5
 2e Deen and chapter of the cathedral church of Hereford.
 RECEIPT for twenty-five pounds three shillings and eleven pence three farthings in respect of the moiety of this tenth for the term of St. Martin, yig. for the second and last term of the fourth year of the aforesaid period offour years. Given st Hereford.
 Latin.
