No. 1069. 1340, April 15. NOTARIAL INSTRUMENT drawn up and attested by John de Beautre, clerk of the diocese of Worcester, notary public, in which Henry de Ustenore, a poor kinsman of dominus Richard de Swynefeud' formerly bishop of Hereford, then in the castle of dominus Adam, bishop of Winchester, at Faraham in the diocese of Winchester, acknowledged the receipt of a certain sum of money as residuary legacy under the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, paid, to him by dominus Roger de Breynton, executor of the will of the said dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford. Witnesses: magistri John de Orleton, canon of Hereford, and John de Usk', official of Winchester. Latin.
