No. 61. 1310, Saturday next after the Feast of St. Barnabas the Apostle [June 13]. 1. Roger de Breyntone, clerk; 2. Richard de Bromptone, vicar of the church of Yauesore. CONDITION of a bond in ten and a half marks for the quiet possession of a messuage with gardens, etc., adjoining, lying between the tenement of dominus Wiliiam Le Ioeuene and the tenement of Matilda Hiche(rn?)ones, which the said Roger bought of tho said Richard in the vill of Chirchebreyntone. Given at Hereford. Latin.
