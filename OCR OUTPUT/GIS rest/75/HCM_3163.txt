 WOTARIAL INSTRUMENT drawn up and attested by — de la More, clerk of the diocese of Exeter, notary public, recording a receipt given by Alisnora, wife of Roger Racy of ae and Richard, Roger, Juliana, and Alice, her children. rough their proectors the said Roger Reey and Richerd 4e Aust, to dazinus Roger ce Breynton', canon of Hereford, for the legacies beaueathed to them by —2dnus Richard de Swynesfeld', late bishop of Hereford, who was their kinsman.
 Witnesses: John de Munstrewrth' and John de Stanford’,926.
