HCM 661
1305/6, Saturday next after the Feast of St. Gregory the Pope [March 19].
AGREEMENT between the dean and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford and Henry le Waleys de Magna Marcleye whereby the former leased to the said Henry all the lands and tenements which they had in pledge of Geoffrey de Sancto Albano for the payment of forty marks, according to the statute for the levying of the debts of merchants, until the said debt and expenses are paid in full. Rent: twenty shillings annually to be paid by the said Henry to the dean and chapter during the term of the lease. Given in the cathedral church of Hereford. Latin.
