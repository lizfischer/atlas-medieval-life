No. 1040. 1317, Saturday next after the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 14]. RECEIPT from Richard called le Mareschal to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford and one of the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty shillings which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin. Two seals, one fragmentary.
