RBLR 2202
1322.3.19
Mar. 19 — The Bishop gives to Roger de Breinton, Rector of Rock, letters freeing him from liability to render any further account of his custody of the  Bishop's goods. Memorandum quod xixº die mensis Marcii , anno Domini M°CCC°xxiº , fecit dominus Rogero de Breynton, Rectori ecclesie de Aka, aquitanciam de recepta et expensa bonorum suorum eum de iterato reddendo compoto absolvens per litteras juxta seriem litterarum prius factarum Waltero Carles.
