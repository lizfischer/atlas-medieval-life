Oct. 2—Mandate    to the Dean   of Pontesbury to allow  Thomas  de 
                     Brompton  to dispose of a  fourth part of  the fruits of the 
                     Church;  the  Btshop will give directions as to  the residue 
                     before the next Ordination. 

                  RELAXACIO   SEQUESTRI  DE  WorpyNn.—Item    dictis die, et loco, 
              ac anno, mandavimus  Decano  de Pontesbury  quod permitteret ipsum 
              custodem  et  suos! administrare de  quarta parte  fructuum  ipsius 
              ecclesie, et quod citra nostros proximos Ordines  celebrandos ordi- 
              nabimus  aliud de eisdem.
