Request  to William  de la Greve  to make  out the  compotus-rolls for 
                    the See, as in the past, and to come to an agreement respecting 
                    the tenths of four years with John de Clare, who will have  the

                    paid  out.   The  question who  shall pay   what  was  due  in 
                    Bishop  John’s time will be matter for  discussion.  He is not 
                    himself in fault, but he will not blame his predecessor.
