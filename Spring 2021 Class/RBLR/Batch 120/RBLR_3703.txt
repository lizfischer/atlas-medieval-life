RBLR 3703
1337.3.10
March 10. Westminster. Licence for the alienation in mortmain by Master Roger de Breynton, clerk, to Thomas, bishop of Hereford, of three messuages, a mill, a carucate of land and 6 acres of meadow, in Cloppeleye, Sevenhampton and Whitebourn, to celebrate yearly the obit of Richard de Swynesfeld, late bishop of the said place, in the bishop's chapel, on the day of his anniversary, the morrow of St. Gregory the Pope. By fine of 40s. Hereford.
