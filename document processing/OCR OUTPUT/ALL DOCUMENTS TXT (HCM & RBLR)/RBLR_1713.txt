No. 1005. 1317, Thursday next after the Feast of the Invention of the Holy Cross [May 5]. RECEIPT from Elianora, wife of Roger Racy to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford, one of the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for one robe with five garments and hoods, this robe being lined with black sindon; which robe was bequeathed to her by the said dominus Richard in his will. Given at Bos'. Latin. Two seals.
