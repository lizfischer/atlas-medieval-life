HCM 2217
1308, 5 Kalends of January [December 28].
NOTIFICATION by Walter de Keyrlyon, priest, presented to the vacent vicarage of the church of St. Gundley in Newport (in novo burgo) in the diocese of Llandaff by the abbot and convent of the monastery of St. Peter's, Gloucester, that he swears an oath of obedience to the monastery and patrons and undertakes to pay an annual pension due from the said vicarage and to protect the church. Given at Gloucester. Latin.
