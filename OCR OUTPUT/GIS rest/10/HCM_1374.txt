HCM 1374
1325, April 12.
. 
 RECEIPT given by Adam, bishop of Hereford, to the dean and chapter of the church of Hereford for eight marks received by the hard of Nicholas de Kserwent, the bishop's clerk, for procurations arising from his vicitetion in the chur ches of Baysham and Dudlebury during the year 1324. Given at Hereford.
 Latin. Broken seal.
