1305, Monday the morrow of Easter [April 19].
1. John de Bristoll', clerk;
2. Abbot and convent of St. Peter's, Gloucester. 

RECEIPT for sixty shillings due to the said John at a specified term, and of another sixty shillings due to him at a specified term, out of an annual pension of twelve pounds due to him during his lifetime. Given at Gloucester.
