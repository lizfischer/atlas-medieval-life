HCM 28
1308, Monday next after the Feast of St. Luke the Evangelist [October 21].
1. Bartholomew le Especer de Hereford'; 2. Dominus William de Broyntone, chaplain, and Margery, his sister. GRANT of fifty-two and a half acres of arable land in the villl of Chirchebreyntone which the grantor held of the dean and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert, Hereford. Given at Hereford. Witnesses: John de la Barewe, John de la Felde, John Lightfot, John de Seuenak', Hugh de Tybertone, Phillip de Ipre, Hugh Ives, William Lethenard', Hugh le Shipward', Hugh de la Stone, John le Bole the clerk. Latin.
