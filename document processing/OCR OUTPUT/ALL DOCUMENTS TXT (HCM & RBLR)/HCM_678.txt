HCM 678
1314, October 14.
1. Walter son of Henry le Reue de Waltone; 2. John Iwayn son of William de Broumia(?).
GRANT by consent of Alice daughter of Richard le Mason de Lenhales, his wife, of one messuage with one toft in Deweswalle lying between the land formerly of dominus John de Turbeuill' and the land of the said John Iwayn and Roger son of Henry le mason and extending from the land formerly of the said dominus John de Turbeuill' as far as the land of the said John Iwayn.
Witnesses: John de Badesshawe, William le Calewe, William Mangaund, John Vyncent, Nicholas atte Walle. Latin.
