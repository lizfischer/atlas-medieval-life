HCM 2668
1306, April.
1. Robert de Cotinton', clerk of the dean and chapter of Hereford; 2. The said dean and chapter by the hand of W. the Young, their receiver. QUITTANCE for twenty shillings due by reason of his office, wage for the term of the Annunciation, 1306 (34 Edw. I). Given at Hereford. Latin. Seal.
