No. 1016. 1317, Tuesday next after the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 17]. RECEIPT from Henry de Wyk' to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty shillings which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin.
