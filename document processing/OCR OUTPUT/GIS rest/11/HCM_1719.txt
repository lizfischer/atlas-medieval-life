HCM 1719
‘Barly 14th century.
1. Geoffrey de la Wode of Ledebur's 2. William Woderoue,
 GRANT, in consideration of twenty-eight shillings, of half a burgage of his lend with ite buildings and oe lying in Subt ende in the vill o:
 bur’ between the land of Ralph Berde end that of Isabel Russel, and extending from the highway to the land of the rector of the gupertor aula of Ledebur',
 Witnesses: Roger le 3k : po le Bottlar, John Cayss, Ralph Berde, William de la Pole,
 Jatin,65.
