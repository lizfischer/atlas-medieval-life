No. 1031. 1317, Monday next after the Feast of Saint John ante portam latiam [May 9]. RECEIPT from John de Tib'ton'; once subcubicularius of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, to the executors of the will of the said dominus Richard, for a bed belonging t o the latter with all its raiment, of value four pounds two shillings and six ten shillings as his annual stipend according to the provisions of the will of the said dominus Richard Given at Hereford. Latin.
