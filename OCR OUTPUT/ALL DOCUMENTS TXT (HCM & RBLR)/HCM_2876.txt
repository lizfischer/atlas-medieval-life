HCM 2876
1313/4, February 10,
1. Ralph le Baron of Wydemarsmore; 2. Dominus Hugh de Browse, penitentiary of the church of Hereford. GRANT of two acres of land lying together in Gerlecroftes between the land of the grantee and the land of Thomas Thoubaud, and extending from the land of the said Thomas as far as the land of John Pride. Witnesses: John Pride, Thomas Theubaud, Walter Roggers, John Thrum, Walter le Brut, Hugh Troue, John de Catfeld, clerk, and others. Latin.
