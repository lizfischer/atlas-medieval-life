HCM 679
LEarly 14th century.
1. Roger le ms 2 John Iweyn, erk.
 GRANT of one acre of arable land in Doweswalle lying in Caldewsallesfeld between the land of Thomas Atenasse and the land of John the clerk and extending from the land of the seid John the clerk as far as the forest of the king.
 Witnesses: Edmund le Waleys, Thomas de la Barre William de la Calewe, Nicholas tweyn de Hereford' Sonn son of Roger Iweyn of the same, John Vyncent, Nicholas de la Hulle, Symon de Lodelawe, clerk.
