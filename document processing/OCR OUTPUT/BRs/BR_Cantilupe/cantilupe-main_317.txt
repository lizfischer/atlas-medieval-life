Apr.  1.—Walter  de  Ludlow,  deacon, presented by  the Chapter, has 
                     letters of institution to Diddlebury and mandate of induction. 

                  DopELEesury.—Memorandum      quod  Kalendis Aprilis, apud Erdes- 
              lege, habuit Walterus de  Lodelowe,  diaconus, litteram institucionis 
              vicarie de Dodelebury,  ad  presentacionem  Capituli Herefordensis, 
              verorum  patronorum   ejusdem.   Et  ipso  die fuerat  demandatum 
              Officiali quod poneret eundem,  etc.
