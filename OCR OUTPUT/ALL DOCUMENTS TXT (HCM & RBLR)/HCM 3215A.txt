No. 3215.A. 1315, the morrow of the Feast of the Assumption of the Blessed Mary [August 16]

1. William son of Adam Hanlyn;
2. Lucia le Shipward.

GRANT, in consideration of twenty shillings, of one parcel of his messuage in Goodrich CAstell. Given at Goodrich CAstell. Latin. Copy dated 15th century.
