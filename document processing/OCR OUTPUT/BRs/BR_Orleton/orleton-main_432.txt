July 14.—End   of an  entry (the earlier portion being lost) in which 
                          the Bishop, as tt would seem, had declared a prebendal por- 
                          tion or portions in some Church of the Diocese to be immune 
                         from  the cure of souls.  On  the same day  and  in the same 
                         form,  mutatis  nominibus,  the prebendal  portions  held by 
                          Gilbert de Middleton and  Thomas  de  Orleton in the Church 
                          of Ledbury  were also declared to be immune   from  the cure 
                          of souls.
