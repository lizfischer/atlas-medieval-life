No. 1048. 1317, Monday the morrow of the Feast of the Apostles Philip and James [May 2]. RECEIPT from Johanna de Gloucestria, relict of William Vynt, formerly citizen of Hereford, to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford and one of the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for half a mark and a coverlet (coopertorium) of russet lined (furratum) with rabbit skins, waich the said dominus Richard bequeathed to her in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin.
