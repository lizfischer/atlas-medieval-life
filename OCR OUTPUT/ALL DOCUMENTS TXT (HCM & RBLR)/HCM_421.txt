HCM 421
1300, Monday next before the feast of St.
,  George the Martyr | April 18]. 1. John called de Stanedichs', clerk; 2e Walter le Brut of Pychenecoumbe.
 AGREEMENT, on payment of twelve marks, for the reversion of land oe between Colverhulle and Blakewelle in the field calied Cowube, areble land lying between the pasture called Oxelesewe and the way called Weynwey near Godeshalueswelle, and a wood between Catwey and Taylesclyne. Given at Pychenecoumbe.
 Laila. S@al.
