HCM 1024
1317, April 26.
RECEIPT from Richard de Oxindenn' to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford and one of the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for five marks in part payment of ten marks which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin. Two seals both broken at the edges.
