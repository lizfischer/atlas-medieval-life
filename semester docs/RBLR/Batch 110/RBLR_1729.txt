No. 2602, 1317, Tuesday next after the Feast of the Ascension [May 17]. 1. John de Scheluing'; 2. Executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, late bishop of Hereford. RECEIPT for one hundred shillings bequeathed to him by the bishop in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin. Two seals.
