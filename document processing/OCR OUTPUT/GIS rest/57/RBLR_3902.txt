1339.3.5
18 5 March 1339 Hillingdon. Mandate instructing  the archdeacon of Worcester, in accordance with the terms of a royal writ, dated 16 February, requesting help against French, to call together the clergy of his archdeaconry on the Monday after Palm Sunday [i.e. 22 March] in the pansh church of Winchcombe being about the centre of the diocese. A similar mandate was sent to the archdeacon of Gloucester.
