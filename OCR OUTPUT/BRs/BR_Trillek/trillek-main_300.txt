Nov.  19.—Memorandum     that  the bishop  buried Sir  Lawrence  de 
                         Lodelowe,  knight, in the Carmelite  church at  Ludlow  and 
                         received and  took away   with him   two horses, armour  jn 
                         two  men, and  13s. 6d., offerings made at the mass  he then 
                         celebrated, without demur  on the part  of the friars.
