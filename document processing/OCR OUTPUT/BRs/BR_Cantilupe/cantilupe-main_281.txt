Nov.  9.—Mandate    to  the Dean  of  Pontesbury  to ctte Robert de 
                          Stoke,  portionist of Westbury, and the Rectors of Worthen and 
                          Pontesbury to appear  before the Bishop. 

                       Crracto RectoruM  DE ‘WestsBuRY, WorTHYN,  ET PonTEsBURY.— 
                  Thomas,   etc., Decano de  Pontesbury  salutem,  etc.  Mandamus 
                  vobis firmiter injungentes quatinus peremptorie citetis magistrum 
                  Robertum   de  Stoke, porcionarium  ecclesie de  Westbury,  et de 
                  Worthyn   ac de Pontesbury ecclesiarum Rectores, quod compareant 
                  coram  nobis, ubicunque,  etc., proximo die juridico post Festum 
                  Beate  Katerine, Virginis et  Martiris, etc. Qualiter autem,  etc. 
                  Datum   apud Chetindone, v Idus  Novembris.               .
