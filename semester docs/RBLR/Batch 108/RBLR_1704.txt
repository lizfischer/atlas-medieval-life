No, 1015. 1317, the day of the blessed Vitalis the martyr [April 28], RECEIPT from Katerina, prioress of Acornebury, and of the convent in the same place, to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford, and to the other executors of the will of dominus Richard, formerly bishop of Hereford, for five marks which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to her in his will . Given in the chapter-house of Acornebury. Latin.
