RBLR 2802
1328.4.14
14 April [1328]. Cleeve. Commission to Ds. Walter Carles and Ds. Roger de Breyntone, canons of Hereford. The bishop authorises them (vices nostras committimus) to make a valuation of his good and chattels in the manors of [his successor], Thomas [Charlton], bishop of Hereford [1327-44], to whom they are to be delievered in part payment of the five hundred marks owed to him -- or in full payment should the value be sufficient.
