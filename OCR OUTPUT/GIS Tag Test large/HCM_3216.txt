HCM 3216
1349/50, January 20.
AGREEMENT made between the dean and chapter of Hereford and the abbot and convent of Redyng' concerning the establishment and continuance of a chantry of one secular chaplain in the conventual church of Leominster in the diocese of Hereford to sing for the souls of {William le Poer?j, and his ancestors daily. The ssid abbot and convent of Redyng' are bound to establish this chentry in return for lands and tenements at Le Hyde Poer fin the lordship of Leominster, and give a bond in one hundred pounds to the said dean and chapter. Erenche Seale Jom end faded. | a |958.
