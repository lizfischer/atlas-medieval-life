HCM 1854
1317, June 10.
MANDATE of Edward II directed to A[dam], bishop of Hereford, and to the dean and chapter of Hereford, reciting that Pope John XXII had granted to him all the moneys collected in his kingdom for the first year of the six-yearly tenth imposed by Pope Clement V, and commanding the said bishop and dean and chapter to deliver the moneys already collected by them to the Exchequer on Wednesday next before the feast of the Nativity of St. John the Baptist, and to collect the remainder of the moneys as soon as possible and to deliver the same to the Exchequer within three weeks of the said feast. Given at Westminster. Latin. [See Celendar of Close Rolls, 1313-18, pp.479-80].
