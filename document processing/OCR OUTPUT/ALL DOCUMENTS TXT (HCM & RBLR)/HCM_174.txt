HCM 174
1312, Tuesday the morrow after the Feast of St. Leonard the Abbot [November 7].
1. Robert Sauage;  2. Phillip le Werour. GRANT of a curtilage lying in Brutone strete in the suburb of Hereford between the land of John le Werour and the land of Walter the fisherman (piscator) of Retherwas and extending from the land of John de la Barwe as far as the green way (viridam viam) of the king. Witnesses: Adam le Porter, bailiff of the fee, Nicholas de Werham, Walter Brond, John Elys, Walter de Hope. Latin.
