1. Ralph le Baroun of wydemersmore; 2e William in le woch of Wydemarsmore.
 GRAIT in fee-ferm of two acres six selions in Gerlecroft lying between the lsnd formerly of Welter de Euesham, cheplain, and the grentor's land, and extending from the land of Thomas le Broy as far as the uddeldich.
 witnesses: llenry de Lugwardin, Welter son of Roger, Hugh Chark', Ralph Judde, Robert Chark, Phillip de Holemar', clerk, Simon de Lodelawe, clerk.
