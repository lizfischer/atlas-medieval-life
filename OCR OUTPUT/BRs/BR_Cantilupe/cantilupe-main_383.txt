Oct. 10.—Robert  Basset, presented by Robert de Tureville, Master of 
                         the Knights  Templars in England,  is instituted to the vicarage 
                         of Cardington.  Mandate   of induction. 

                      CaRDYNTONE.—Item      eisdem   die,  loco, et  anno,   dominus 
                  Robertus  Basset, capellanus, fuerat institutus in vicaria de Card- 
                  ingtone, ad  quam   presentabatur  per  religiosum  virum,  fratrem 
                  Robertum   de  Tureville, Militum   Templi  in  Anglia  Magistrum, 
                  verum   patronum   ipsius.  Et  tune  Officiali demandabatur  quod 
                  poneret  eum, etc.
