No. 2850. [circa 1297-1298].

PRESENTMENT of a jury in full court in the presence of John Lightfot, then seneschal of the dean and chapter of Hereford. [References to Gerard de Eylesford and to the obit of Hugh Folyot]. 
On the dorse: Estreat of the court of Hamme Lacy held on Thursday next before the Feast of the Purification of the Blessed Mary, 26 Edward [I].
