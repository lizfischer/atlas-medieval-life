HCM 2584
1306, Thursday next after the Feast of St. Michael [October 6].
1. William de Henouere; 2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, by the hands of dominus William the Young.
QUITTANCE for ten shillings in which they were bound to him in respect of his fee for the term of St. Michael, 34 Edw. [I]. Given at Henouere.
Latin.
