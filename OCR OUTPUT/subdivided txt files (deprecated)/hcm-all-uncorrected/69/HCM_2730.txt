HCM 2730
1330, November 10,
. 
 1. Thomas de Boleye and Philip de la More, appointed by Thomas, bishop of Hereford, chief collector in his diocese, to be sub-collectors of the tenth for four years lately imposed on the oor by Pope John XXII;
 2. Dean and chapter of the cathedral church of Hereford. QUITTANCE for twenty-five pounds three shillings and
 eleven pence halfpenny in respect of the moiety of the
 said tenth of their property in the aforessid diocese of
 Hereford for the second term of the first year of the said
 period of four years, yig. for the term of St. Martin in
 the present year. biven at Hereford.
 Latin.
