HCM 681
1316/7, Saturday next after the Feast of [?St. Hillary] [January 15].
1. Sibil formerly wife of William Reed, widow; 2. John Iwayn, clerk. RELEASE of lands and tenements which the said John held in the vills of Deuswalle, Wormetone and Deusweschirche. Given at Hereford. Witnesses: Edmund le Walsse, John de Baddesawe, Thomas Aubrey, William le Calew[e], William Mangant, John Vincent, Roger Iwayn. Latin. Seal. Torn.
