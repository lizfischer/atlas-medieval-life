1336/7, Sunday next sfter the Feast of St.
Hillary LJanuary 15]. 1. John | Trilleck], bishop of Hereford; 2 Dominus Andrew de Wermenstre, clerk.
 LEASE for life of lands and tenements which William atte Culne, Madoc Prat' and Robert de Cuple held of the lessor in villenage in Cuple in Bromyord fforeyn.
 Rents one rose annually.
 Witnesses: John de La Were, William Broun, John de Pencombe, John Broun de la Hyde, John de Herdewyk'.
 Dated at Bromyord. Latin. Seale
