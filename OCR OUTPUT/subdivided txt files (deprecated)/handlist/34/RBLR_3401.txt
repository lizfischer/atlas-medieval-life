182 21 January 1332 Hartlebury. Commission to Ds. R[oger] de Breynton, archdeacon of Gloucester, for carrying out an exchange of benefices within the diocese. Richard, son of Robert de Castro of Halford (Halford), and Richard de Compton, called 'of Northleach' (de Northlech), vicars respectively of Sherborne (Schirebourn) and Hartpury (Hardepyrie), propose to exchange benefices. Should the enqurry which he is to hold find for presentors and presentees, he is to admit, institute and induct the parties to their respective churches and to receive their oaths of continual residence and canonical obedience. The bishop is to be informed of his proceedings by letters patent.
