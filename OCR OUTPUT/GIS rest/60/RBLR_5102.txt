1351.5.12
4 Id. May. Villeneuve by Avignon. (f. 155d.) To Gervase de Wilford, canon of Salisbury and Southwell, and rector of Bernak, in the diocese of Lincoln. Confirmation of the church of Dodyngton, in the diocese of Ely, which he obtained by authority of the ordinary, Thomas, bishop of Ely, on the death of Roger de Brayngton, but which he fears was reserved to the pope. [Cal. Pet. i. 208.]
