.1326, post September 19.
. 
 PRECEPT of Richard Wroth', guatos of the hundred of Wormelowe; to his bailiffs of the said hundred, to give seisin to Alice, daughter of William Rokulf, of a ones of forty acres of land and a mill in Deweswalle. The said Alice has recovered the said messuage against John de Brideport' and Isabella, his wife, through an sssize between them held before the sald Richard at the Hundred of Wormelowe on Friday next after the Feast of the Exaltation of the Holy Cross in the above year.
