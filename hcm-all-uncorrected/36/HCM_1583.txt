HCM 1583
1287, the Feast of St. Thomas the Apostle
December 21). 1. John, abbot of St. Peter's Gloucester, and the convent of the same place; 2. John the clerk.
 LEASE for the life of the said John and that of his sister, of a fardel of land with its appurtenances in the vill of cutberley.
 Rent: two shillings end six pence annually at four specified terms. Given at Gloucester.
 Witnesses: John de Stabulo, John and Richard de Bosco, William de Marisco, Luke le Chamberlay.
 Latin.582.
