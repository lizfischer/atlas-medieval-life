The  Precentor  is instructed not to give occasion of  appeal to the 
                     Abbot of Reading  or others, and to ascertain through Antony 
                     Beke tf the King intends to cross the Channel this summer. 

                  Precentor1.—Item   mandabatur  in quadam  littera domino Pre- 
             centori quod nec  domino  Abbati Radingie nec  alii daret materiam 
             appellandi, et quod  mitteret ad dominum   A(ntonium]   Beke, ubi 
             Dominus   Rex  foret in quindena Sancte Trinitatis, anno LXXXI°, 
             ad  sciendum  ab  ipso si Rex  debeat  hac  estate transfretare, et

super hoc et aliis certificaret Dominum per aliquem  de familiaribus, 
                   excepto Bruselaunce.
