HCM 384
1315/6, Tuesday next after the Feast of St. Valentine [February 17].
1. John Batun; 2. Walter son of William Hathewy of Oxenhale.
GRANT in fee-farm of a parcel of land in Oxenhale lying in the field called Middelfeld in breadth between the land of William le Gondre and the land of William le Tornour and in length from the land of Roger the marshall as far as the land of Roger le Webbe of Newent. Given at Oxenhale.
Witnesses: Roger the marshall, Walter de la More, Roger de la Ghildhalle, John Gille, William Bollok.
Latin.
