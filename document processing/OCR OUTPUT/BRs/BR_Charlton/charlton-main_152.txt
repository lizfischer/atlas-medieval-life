April 6.—Commission    to William   de Fowehope,  Adam    Carbonel, 
                           canons, and John  Rees, rector of Whitbourne, to hold a court 
                           for the trial of Richard Moniword, citizen of Hereford, accused 
                           of heresy. 

               A.D. 1336.
