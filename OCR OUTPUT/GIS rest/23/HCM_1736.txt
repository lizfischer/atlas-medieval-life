HCM 1736
1305, Octave of the Feast of St. John the Baptist [July 1].
1. Roger de la Grene de Bulleye and Alice his wife, plaintiffs; 
2. John de Acle, chaplain, deforciant. 

FINAL CONCORD made in the king's court at Westuinster before Ralph de Hengham, William de Bereford', Elias de Bekynham, Peter Malorre, William Howard' and Lambert de Trikingham, justices,concerning one messuage, two hundred acres of land, eight acres of meadow and an . annual rent of five marks with their appurtenances in Bulleye. The deforciant, in consideration of twenty pounds, acknowledges the said properties to belong to the plaintiffs.
Latin.
