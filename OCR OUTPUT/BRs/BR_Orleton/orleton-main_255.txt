Nov. 9—The   Bishop of Hereford commissions the Bishop of Lincoln 
                         to act for him in the matter of an exchange between Thomas 
                         de  Portington, Rector of Taynton, and Walter de Boneville, 
                         Rector of  Welby.

                            dum   resignacionem  Thome   de  Portingtone, Rectoris  ecclesie de
