No. 1041. 1317, Saturday next before the Feast of Saint Ethelbert the king and martyr [May 14]. RECEIPT from John de Barewe to domini John de Kemeseye, John de la Felde, magister Robert de Icclesham, canons of Hereford, and dominus Roger de Breynton', executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynesfeld, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty shillings which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin.
