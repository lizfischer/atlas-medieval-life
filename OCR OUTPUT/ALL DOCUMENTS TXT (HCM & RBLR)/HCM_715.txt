HCM 715
1317/8, February 26.
NOTARIAL INSTRUMENT, drawn up and attested by Walter de Maddeleye, clerk of the diocese of Hereford, notary public, recording the acknowledgment of the chief parishioners of Maddeleye before the dean and chapter of Hereford and the bishop's official that they have no right to the offerings made before the statue of the Virgin Mary in the church of Maddeleye which were intended for the fabric of the new chancel. Latin. [Printed in W. W. Capes, Charters and Records of Hereford Cathedral, pp.183-4].
