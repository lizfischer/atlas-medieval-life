April 18.—Mandate  to the bishop to levy from the goods of William 
                                   Golafre, parson of Awre, one  hundred  and  twenty pounds, 
                                  due  to John  de Markeleye. 

                            A.D.  1836.

                              8. Michaelis  proximo   sequenti quadraginta   libras, et in festo  S.
