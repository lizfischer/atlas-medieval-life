HCM 2888
1335, May 8,
1. William, prior, and the convent of the monastery of St. Mary of Lanthon' Auta Gloucester; 2. Stephen, dean, and the chapter of the church of Hereford.
 ASSIGNMENT for the maintenance of choristers of an annual pension of half a mark issuing from the church of Kyngton', appropriated to the assignors by Thomas, bishop of the church of Hereford. Given at Lanthon'.
 Latin.
