HCM 1521
1330, Monday, the Feast of St. George
the martyr | April 23].
 DEFEASANCE to a bond in twenty pounds made between John, abbot, sand the convent of St. Peter's, Gloucester, and John de Ypre. If the said abbot and convent shall enjoy the possession of certain lands and tenements for the full term of thirty years according to a demise made to them by the said John de Ypre, the bond shall be void. Otherwise, it shall retain its force. Given at Gloucester.
 Latin.
