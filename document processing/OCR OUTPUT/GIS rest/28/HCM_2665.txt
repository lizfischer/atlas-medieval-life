1305, in the Vigil of the Feast of St. Michael [Sept. 28].
1. Adam de Barewe, clerk of the dean and chapter of Hereford; 2. Dominus William de Foston', chaplain end receiver of the said dean and chapter. RECEIPT for forty shillings in respect of his stipend for the term of the Annunciation, 1305, and for the term of St. Michael next following. Latin.
