No 1038. 1317/8, Wednesday the next after the Feast of Saint Benedict the abbot [March 22]. RECEIPT from Thomas, vicar of the church of Bocton' in the diocese of Canterbury, to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swinefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for two marks sterling to be distributed among the poor parishioners of the church of Bocton'; which money the said dominus Richard bequeathed to them in his will. Given at Canterbury. Latin.
