HCM 2869
1301, Tuesday, the day after the Feast
of St. Leodegar (Oct. 3]. 1. John de Aqua Blenea, dean of the church of Hereford; 2. ae of Hereford, an the hand of dominug William c: a the Young, chaplain.
 QUITTANCE for nine shillings of an annual payment due from the four manors of the aforesaid church, vize in respect of Peter's Pence. Given at Hereford.
 Latin.
