HCM 2722
1310/11, February 3.
1. Bishops of Lincoln and London, appointed collectors in the whole of England, Scotland, Wales and Ireland of the tenth imposed for three years by Pope Clement V for the Holy Land;
2. Dean and chapter of Hereford by the hand of dominus John de Wytteney, their clerk and proctor.
QUITTANCE for sixty pounds in respect of the sum of the said tenth collected by them for the second term of the second year of the said period of three years. Given at London.
Latin.
