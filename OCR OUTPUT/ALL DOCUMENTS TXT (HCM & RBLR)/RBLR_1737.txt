1317.7.12
No. 1002. 1317, Tuesday next after the feast of the Translation of the blessed Benedict, the abbot [July 12]. RECEIPT from Adam de Herwynton', to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for one gold ring with sapphire, with which the said dominus Richard was consecrated as bishop, and which he bequeathed to the said Adam in his will. Given at London. Latin. Chipped seal.
