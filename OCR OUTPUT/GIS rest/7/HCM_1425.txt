HCM 1425
1289, 13 Kal. August LJuly 20.
. 
 INDULGENCE offered by We, bishop of Salisbury, granting a relaxation of twenty days of penance to penitents who make a pilgrimage to the tomb of votes Thomas, et bishop of Hereford, in the church of Hereford, and contribute to the maintenance of the fabric of that church. Given at Hereford.
 Latin. Broken Seal.
