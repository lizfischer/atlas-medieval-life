HCM 342
1329, Monday next before the Feast of St.
| Dunstan the Archbishop (May 15]. 1. Hugh Odyarn de Stanedisch; 2. Peter Engelond de Morton’. RELEASE of lands which the said Peter held in the lordship of Stenédisch for life. Given at Stanedisch. Witnesses: Robert Mordefrait, Henry le ket, John le Walsche de Husmerleie, Richard atte Hoke, stephen le Granger, william Davy, John Hathemar', Simon de Fromelode, Nicholas de Salle. | Latin. |
