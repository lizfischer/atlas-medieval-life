le Alice de Trompelton'; 2e. William lie Crompe.
 GRANT in fee-farm, in consideration of sixteen shillings, of one acre of land In the territory of Deuschireche between the grantor's land and the land of John de Fonte and extending above the road leading from Deuswalle to Essildebrugge and at the other emi above the grantor's land.
 Rents two pence annually.
 Witnesses: Walter de la Barre, wae Mie renee Robert le Petit, Richard de Achung', John Boter, Willig Mangeunt, Hugh felle, Adem de Fowehope, clerk.
