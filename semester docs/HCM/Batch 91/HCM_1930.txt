HCM 1930
1313, October 7.
MANDATE of Edward [II] to the dean and chapter of the dean and chapter of Hereford, sub-collectors of the annual tenth granted to the King by the Pope, that, since he owes a certain sum of a Menent Francisk' and his fellow-merchants of the society of Bardi [of Florence], the dean and chapter shall not pay any of the moneys of the said tenth to anyone except the said merchants or the chief collectors of the tenth until the said debt is fully satisfied. Given at Westminster. French.
