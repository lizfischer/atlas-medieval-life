HCM 2257-em
1306, December 2.
MANDATE from Richard [de Swinfield], bishop of Hereford, to the dean and chapter of Hereford, reciting the king's writ dated 3 November 1306 summoning the bishop and the dean and chapter to the parliament at Carlisle in the octaves of St. Hillary next following, and ordering the dean and chapter to carry out the instructions therein as they affected them. Dated at Bosebur'.
Latin.
