No. 35. 1316, April 4. 1. Roger de Breynton', clerk; 2. Magister Gilbert de Reigate, rector of the church of Brugges Solers. AGREEMENT for the annulment of the grant specified in No. 33 of even date, subject to the payment of one hundred shillings, reserving a term of five years in a croft formerly of Nicholas de la Calewe, together with a bond in ten marks for the observance of covenants. Given at Hereford. Latin.
