HCM 2597
[1310], October 20.
1. Bishops of Lincoln and London, collectors in the whole of England, Scotland, Ireland, and Wales of the tenth lately imposed for three years by Pope Clement V;
2. Dean and chapter [of Hereford], subcollectors, by the hands of dominus John de Whitteneye, their clerk and proctor.
 QUITTANCE for two hundred pounds for the second year of the said period of three years, Given at London. Latin. Mutilated.
