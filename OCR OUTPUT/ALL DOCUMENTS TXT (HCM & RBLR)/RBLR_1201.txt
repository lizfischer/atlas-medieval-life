No, 553. 1311/2, Thursday next after the Feast of St . Valentine [February 17]. 1. John Lygthfot de Hereford'; 2. John de la Berne. RELEASE of twelve acres of arable land in the field of Wydeden' which he held at farm by the grant of the said John de la Berne. Given at Hereford. Witnesses: Hugh le Schypward', Adam Wyty, Roger Fraunceys, Adam called le clerk, Roger de Breynton'. Latin.
