No. 1013. 1317, Saturday next after the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 14]. RECEIPT from Roger le Barbour to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford, one of the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty shillings sterling which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin.,
