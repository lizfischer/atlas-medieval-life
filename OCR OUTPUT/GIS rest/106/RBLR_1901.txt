No. 34. 1318/9, Sunday the morrow of St. Hilary [January 141]. 1. Gilbert de Reygate, rector of the church of Brugge Solers; 2. Dominus Roger de Breynton', rector of the church of Aka in the diocese of Hereford. RELEASE of lands and tenements in Chyrchebreynton'. Given at Hereford. Witnesses: John de Barewe, then seneschal of the dean and chapter of the church of Hereford, Thomas de la Barre, Adam Lucas, John de Brugge, Walter, his brother, William de Stretton', John Pride, William de la Were, Walter de Leoministr', clerk. Latin.
