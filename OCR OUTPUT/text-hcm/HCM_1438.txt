HCM 1438
1309, Tuesday next before the Feast of St. Kenelm [July 15].
RECEIPT of John Talebot and Alfredus, chaplain, executors of the will of magister Philipp Talebot, formerly canon in the church of Hereford, for eleven and a half marks which they have received of magistri Robert de Glouc', chancellor of the church of Hereford, William de Caple, canon, dominus Hugh de Breosa, penitentiary of the same church, and the chapter of Hereford, in part payment of twenty marks owing to them for the prosecution of the canonisation of dominus Thomas de Cantilupo, formerly bishop of Hereford. Given at Hereford. Latin.
