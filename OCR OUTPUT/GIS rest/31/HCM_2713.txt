HCM 2713
1306, Tuesday next after the Feats of the Annunciation [March 29].
1. John de la Barew', bailiff of the dean and chapter of Hereford; 2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, by the hand of Willian the Young, their receiver. QUITTANCE for twenty shillings in respect of fee for the term of the Annunciation. Given at Hereford. Latin.
