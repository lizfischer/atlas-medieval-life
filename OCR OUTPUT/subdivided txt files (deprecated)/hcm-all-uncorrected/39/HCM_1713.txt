HCM 1713
1326/7, Sunday next after the Feast of the
Purification of the Blessed Mary | February 8].
 BOND of Godfrey Morice and Matilda his wife to preserve unharmed from oll injury and loss goming Sibyl, widow of sms Alen de Plukenet, who has granted to them ell the
 ends and tenements in bs tgeynge in Berkshire which she held as a dowry. Given at La Mersche juxts Yeele.
 Latin.
