1293, April 1.
, 
 MEMORANDUM by John, dean, and the chapter of the church of Hereford, together with John de Swinfeud, treasurer of the same church, concerning the dispute which has arisen between the two parties in respect of their eleims to the offerings of wax at the tomb of petjaue Thomas de Cantilupo, formerly bishop of Hereford. wo parties agree to submit their dispute to the judgment of aa Richard, bishop of Hereford. Given in the chapter-house at Hereford.
 Latin, Jimsealss both broken.
