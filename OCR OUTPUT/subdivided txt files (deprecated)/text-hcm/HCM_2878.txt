HCM 2878
1316, Friday next after the Feast of St. Bartholomew the Apostle [August 27].
RELEASE by John de Kemes', treasurer of Hereford, of dominus Willion Vallet, vicar in the church of Hereford, from the office of sub-treasurer, Given at Bosebur'. Latin. [Printed in W. W. Capes, Charters and Records of Hereford Cathedral, p.183].
