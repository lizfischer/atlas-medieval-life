HCM 1683
1317/8, Feast of St. Benedict the abbot [March 21].
1. John, abbot, and the convent of St, Peter's, Gloucester; 2. John atte Mulle, son of Herbert atte Mulle, formerly villein of the said abbot and convent at Stanleye St. Leonards.
GRANT in fee-farm of that mill with its appurtenances which the said Herbert formerly held, after the death of Edith, mother of the grantee; the said mill to be held by the said John and wife Petronilla.
Rent to the Prior of Stanleye: twenty shillings annually at four specified terms; also way of aid at the Feast of St. Michael four shillings; for two days in hay-turning and weeding and three harvest-services in Autumm six pence at Lammas-day; and for a present due at Christmas six pence at the said Feast. If the said Petronilla survives her husband, she shall not remarry without the consent of the grantors. Given in the chapter-house at Gloucester.
Latin. Seal.
