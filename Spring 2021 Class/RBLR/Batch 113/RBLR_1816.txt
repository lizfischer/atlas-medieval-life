1318.7.18
To Master Hugh de Matiscon, doctor of canon law. Provision of the rectory of Wessington, in the diocese of Durham, void by the cession of Aymo de Sabaudia., who held it as a pluralist without papal dispensation, notwithstanding that Hugh is beneficed out of England. Concurrent mandate to Gerard de Cusancia, canon of London, and two others named.
