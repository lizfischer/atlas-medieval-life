1319.11.4
No, 25, 1319, November 4. 1, Roger le Leche de Hereford'; 2. Dominus Roger de Breynton', rector of the church of Aka. GRANT of arable land in the vill and fields of Chyrchebreynton', viz, nine acres. Given at Hereford. witnesses: Johh de Brugge, William de Stretton', John Iweyn, Hugh Troue, Hugh le Schypward', Adam Hwyty, Walter de Leom', clerk. Latin. Seal.
