No. 1070. 1342, April 5. NOTARIAL INSTRUMENT drawn up and attested by Philip de la More, clerk of the diocese of Exeter, notary public, In which Juliana Scort de Estenore in the diocese of Hereford, a poor kinswoman in secundo gradu consanguinitatis of domlnus Richard de Swynesfeld', formerly bishop of Hereford, then In the household of dominus Roger de Breynton', canon of Hereford and executor of the will of the said bishop acknowledged the receipt from dominus Roger de Breynton' of all the items bequeathed to her in the bishop's will. Mention is made of a clause In the will which concerned the poor kinsmen of the bishop. Witnesses: dominus Simon de Radenore and Richard de Aust'. clerks. Latin.
