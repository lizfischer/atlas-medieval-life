HCM 425
i circa 1300.
1. Walter le Brut of Pychenecumbes 2 John de Stanedis', clerk.
 GRANT, in consideration of one hundred shillings, of a wood between Mussyeshale end the well called Godeshalueswelle and the way called Portweye and a place called tet gees in breadth, and extending in length from the said as far as a way called Schyrengesweye.
 Witnesses: Roger de Ravenhulle, Geoffrey de Stonhouse, Stephen de Masyntone, Henry Ythenard, William de Pydesmore, Walter Carpentar', Henry de Molendino,.
 Latine Seal.
