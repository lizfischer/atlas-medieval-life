No. 1012. 1317, Sunday next after the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 15]. RECEIPT from John de Stildon' to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for half a mark which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to his in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin. Two seals.
