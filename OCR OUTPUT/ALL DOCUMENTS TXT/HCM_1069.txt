HCM 1069
1340, April 15. |
NOTARIAL INSTRUMENT drawn up and attested by John de Beautre, clerk of the diocese of Worcester, notary public, in which Henry de Estenore, a poor kinsman of ouinus Richard de Swynefeud' fornerly bishop of Hereford, then in the castle of gominus Adem, bishop of Winchester, at Farnham in the diocese of Winchester, acknowledged the receipt of a certein sum of money as residuary legacy under the will of adie Richard de Swynefeud, paid to him by Roger de Breynton', executor of the will of the seid Richard de Swynefeud', formerly bishop of Hereford. witnesses: Bend nh John de Orleton', canon of Hereford, and John de Usk', official of Winchester. Latin. .
