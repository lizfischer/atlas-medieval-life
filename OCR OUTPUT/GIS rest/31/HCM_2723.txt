HCM 2723
1315, October 14.
1. Bishops of Lincoln and London, appointed collectors in England, Scotland, Ireland and Wales of the tenth lately imposed for one year by Pope Clement V for the Holy Land;
2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, sub-collectors, by the hand of Nicholas de Yanewrth', clerk, their proctor.
QUITTANCE for seventeen pounds in respect of the sum of the said tenth collected by them for both terms of the said one year tenth. Given at London.
Latin. Two seals.
