Sept. 7.— Custody of the Chapel of Shipton committed to John de Ayno, 
                 acolyte, presented by the Priory of Wenlock. 
               Skyptone.—Septimo   Idus Septembris commissa  est Johanni de 
          Ayno,  acolito, ad capellam de Scyptone per Priorem et Conventum de 
          Wenlac   presentato, custodia ejusdem capelle usque ad  proximum 
          sequens Natale Domini.
