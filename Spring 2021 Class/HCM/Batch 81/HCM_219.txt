HCM 219
1317, April 11.
1. John le Werrur, chaplain; 2. Walter le Bonde. GRANT of a messuage in Hereford lying in the street of Hungreye between the land of Hugh Levioth(?) and the land of Matilda de Magdalona and extending from the highway to the way which is called Bihyndewalle. Witnesses: Richard de Cruce, Thomas Tope, John de la Sale, bailiffs of the king in Hereford, Hugh Leuioth(?), Nicholas de Werham, John de Luwel, Henry le Bakare. Latin. Seal.
