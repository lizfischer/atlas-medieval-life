No. 4. 1316, from St. Michael in three weeks [October 20]. FINAL CONCORD in a fine levied between Roger de Breynton', clerk, plaintiff, and John de la Berne and Sybil his wife, deforciants, when the deforciants acknowledged one messuage and seventy-one acres of land in Chirchebreynton' to be the right of the plaintiffs. Latin.
