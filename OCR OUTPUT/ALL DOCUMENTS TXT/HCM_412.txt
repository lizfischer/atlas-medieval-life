HCM 412
1314, Friday next before the Feast of St. Margaret the Virgin [July 19].
LETTER OF ATTORNEY by Thomas de Aylineston', tailor, of Hereford, to Hugh de Monyton', citizen of Hereford, to receive an annual rent of two shillings from a tenement of Hugh Bade, namely from a shop lying in the city of Hereford between the tenement the said Hugh Bade and the tenement of the said Thomas, and to distrain in case of default. Given at Hereford.
Latin.
