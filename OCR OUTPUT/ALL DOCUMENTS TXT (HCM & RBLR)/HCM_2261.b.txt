HCM 2261.b
1321/2, February 276

 WRIT to the keeper of the land of Gower now in the king's hend on petition of Alice, daughter of William Roeulf, junior, showing that during the late disturbance John de Moubray and his adherents took and slew John Iwayn, whose kinswoman and heir the said Alice is, snd unjustly occupied certain lands pertaining to the said Alice, which are now teken into the king's hand, ordering an inquisition to be made thereon.
 Latin. Copy.
