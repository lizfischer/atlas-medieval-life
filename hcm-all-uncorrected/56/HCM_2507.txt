HCM 2507
1332/3, January 13,
1. eltur! Carles, canon of the church of St, wena bore. Herefords 2. Dean and chapter of the aforesaid church,
 RECEIPT, on taking Homme Lacy to farm, for twenty-four oxen end two draught-animals of the value of thirteen shillings and four pence each, one cart (carecta) and four wagons (pheusteum) hooped with iron (erro ligat') of the value of ten shillings each, four plouchs and four harrows of the value of twelve pence each and three tables, with trestles, in the hall, all of which are to be handed over at the end of the term of the aforesaid farm. Given in the said church of Hereford.
