1300, Saturday, on the morrow of the Feast
of st. John the Baptist (June 25]. 1. William de Henor'; 2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, by the hand of gominus william de Foston', chaplain.
 QUITTANCE for ten shillings in respect of his fee for the term of St. Michael, 1299, and ten shillings for the term of the Annuncistion, 1308, Given at Hereford.
