HCM 1431
1321, 18 Kal. May  April 14.
. 
 INDULGENCE offered by William, archbishop of York snd primate of England, granting a relaxation of forty days of penance to penitents who visit the tomb of Saint Thomas the Confessor, formerly bishop of Hereford, and seek his intercession, snd are present st the celebrations on the Feast Dey of the Confessor which is on the sixth day before the Nones of October each year, or give of their possessions in his honour, or contribute to the maintenance of the fabric, candles, or vestments of the church of Hereford, Given at Cawde.
 Latin.
