No. 1023. 1317, Thursday in the octaves of the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 19]. RECEIPT from Richard de Hamenasch', canon of Hereford, to the executors of the will of dominua Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twelve shillings for the use of his four servants (garciones); which money the said dominus Richard bequeathed to them in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin.
