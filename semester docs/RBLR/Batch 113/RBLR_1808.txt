No. 1029. 1317/8, Wednesday next after the Feast of Saint Benedict the abbot [March 22]. RECEIPT from the prioress and sisters of the hospital of Saint James outside the town of Canterbury to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for forty shillings which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to them in his will. Given at Canterbury. Latin.
