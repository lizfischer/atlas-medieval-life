HCM 2712
1305 the Feast of St. Faith the Virgin [Oct. 6].
1. Williem Valet, sub-treasurer in the church of Hereford; 2. Dean and chapter of the aforesaid church, by the hand of gominus William the Young. QUITTANCE for twenty-four shillings payable each year to the treasurer for the purchase of oil, viz. for term of the Annunciation, 1305, and for the term of St. Michael next following. Given at Hereford. Latin. Seal.
