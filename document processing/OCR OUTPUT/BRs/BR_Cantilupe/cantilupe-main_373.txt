Sept. 27.—Licence  of non-residence to study for one year granted to 
                         John  de Arras,  Rector of Neenton, and  to Thomas   Botterell, 
                         Rector of  Aston Botterell. 

                      LicenciA  stuDENDI.—Memorandum     quod v  Kalendas Octobris, 
                  anno nostre Consecracionis quarto, apud Castrum Episcopi, fuerunt 
                  Johannes de Arraz, Rector ecclesie de Neentone, et Thomas Boterel, 
                  Rector ecclesie de Aston Boterel, licenciati studere a Festo Sancti 
                  Michaelis usque in annum  completum,  in forma communi.
