1316, Monday in the vigil of the Feast of St. Bartholomew, the apostle [August 23].
 1. William Woderoue of Estenouere;
 2. Williom de la Pole of Ledbury.
 GRANT of half a burgage of his land with its buildings and all its appurtenances lying in Suht Ende in the vill of Ledbury between the land of Ralph Berde and that of Geoffrey de la Wode, and extending from the highway to the land of the rector of the upper hall (superioris aule) of Ledbury. Given at Ledbury.
 Witnesses: John Cayss, Ralph le botiler, William Balle, John ate crose, Robert Foliot.
 Latin.
