1333/4, Friday next after the Feast of St.
Matthies the Apostle February 25].
 1. John de Reotherwas, citizen of Hereford;
 2+ Dominus Gilbert Thurgrym, chaplain.
 GRANT of a curtilage in a suburb of Hereford Byhyndecote > dary Meaney the tenement of William de Rugleye and the curtilage of Thomas Vigerous and extending from the way called Byhyndecote as far eas grantor's curtilage. Given at Hereford. ". 
 Witnesses: John de la Barr', John Blod, Thomas Tope, bailiffs of the king in the city of Hereford, Thomas Tebaud, Thomes le Deygher', Bartholomew Thurgrym, Giles de la Berue, Hugh the clerk.
