HCM 2748
1306, Saturday, the day after the Feast of St. Kalixtus [October 15].
1. William Valet, subtreasurer of the church of Hereford; 2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, by the hand of dominus William the Young, their receiver.
QUITTANCE for twenty-four shillings due to the treasurer for the purchase of oil for the term of the Annunciation and for the term of St. Michael next following. Given at Hereford.
Latin.
