Feb. 14—The    Bishop commissions William de Fownhope  to act for 
                         him  in  the matter of  John, son  of Richard  Fullon, of 
                         Fownhope,    presented to the manorial Chantry of Fownhope 
                         by Roger   de Chandos.  On  the following day  the Bishop 
                         ordained the same  John to the priesthood.
