HCM 638
1347, Monday next after the Feast of
the Apostles Peter and Peul | July 2]. 1. John de Homptone; 2. John le Spencer de Moretone.
 GRANT of an secre of land of two selions lying in Luide Godefray in le Middelfeld, one of the selions lying between the land of Roger de Lugwardyn on both sides and the other ...e.e. lying at the end of the said selion. Given at Luide Godefray.
 Witnesses: Roger de Hulle, Henry le Miners, Hamon le Brut, William de Shelwyk', Hugh ecccecse
 Latin. Seale Lartly faded.
