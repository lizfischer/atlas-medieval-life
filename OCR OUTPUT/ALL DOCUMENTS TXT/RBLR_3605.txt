1336.12.23
Dec. 23. Doncaster. Licence for the dean and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert, Hereford, to acquire in mortmain land and rent, not held in chief, to the yearly value of 20l., to establish chantries and other alms in that church for the souls of Richard, their late bishop; and the faithful departed. By p.s.
