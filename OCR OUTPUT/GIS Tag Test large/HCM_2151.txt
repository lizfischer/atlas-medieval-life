HCM 2151
1309, June 10.
CONFIRMATION by Richard [Swinfield], bishop of Hereford, of the appropriation to the dean and chapter of Hereford of the churches of Holemare, Longastauton', and la Pipe, which John le Bretun, formerly bishop of Hereford, had granted to them in a defective instrument. Given at Bosebur'. Latin. Chipped seal.
