HCM 694
Lgirea 1300.
1. Stephen son of John de wormetone; 2e Stephen son of William Payn de Deuiswalle,.
 GRANT in fee-farm, in consideration of three marks, of the moiety of a messusage with nine acres of land, vize, the land which David de Dunre formerly held in the vill of Deuiswalle.
 Rent: two shillings annually to the lord of the fee.
 Witnesses: William Caluuo, Williauw Mangeent, Hugh Helle, John Boter, John son of John Cradoc, John son of Vincent, and Gregory the clerk.
 Latin.
