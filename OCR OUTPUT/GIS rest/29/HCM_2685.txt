HCM 2685
1331, Sunday next before the Feast of
St. Dionysius | October 6]. 1. Roger de Chaundos, sheriff of Hereford; 2. Deen and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford,
 QUITTANCE for eight pounds ten shillings raised from the goods of the said dean and chapter for the use of Giles le Taillour of Hereford by reason of a writ of the king to be returned before the king's justices at Westminster. Given at Hereford.
 Latin.
