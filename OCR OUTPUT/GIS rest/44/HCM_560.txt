HCM 560
1338/9 Monday next after the Feast of
e Epiphany LJanuary 11). 1. John de Asshe son and heir of John de Asshe; 2. Beatrice daughter of William Eseger. GRANT, in connection with his marriage to the said Beatrice, of the manor of Gatesford’ with all appurtenances. Given at Gatesford’. Witnesses: Walter Moton, Walter le Chaumberleyn, Stephen Eseger, Henry de Paners, Williem le Spicer, Richard @irel, John de Solers, David Aubrey, John le Parker. Latin. Seal.
