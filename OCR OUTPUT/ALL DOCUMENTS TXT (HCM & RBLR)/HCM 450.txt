No. 450. [circa 1275]
1. William Clement de Mora Canonieorum;
2. Dominus William de Euesham, chaplain.
GRANT in fee-farm, in consideration of eight shillings, of a fossatum at Mora Canicorum outside Hereford lying cetween the croft of the said William Clement and the croft which the said dominus William bought of Adam son of Osebert and containing inwidth four and a half feet.
Rent: one clove annually.
Witnesses: Hugh the clerk, Thomas Thebaud', William the dyer (tinctor), John Lylye, John Thrum, Ralph Judde , Roger de la feld', William de lecche, clerk.
Latin.
