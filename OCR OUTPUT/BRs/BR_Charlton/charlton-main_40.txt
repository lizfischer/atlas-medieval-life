July  21—The    bishop,  after visitation of the church of Erselane 
                                  (Hardisland),  which  was   appropriated  to the  abbot  and 
                                  convent  of Lyre,  puts  on record  his acceptance of  twelve 
                                  marks  which, as had  been shewn by the proctor of Lyre, was 
                                  the customary payment   to preceding bishops. 

                                 Universis sancte   matris  ecclesie filiis, etc., Thomas,   etc.

                            Herefordensi,  appropriatas  et apprebendatas   jure diocesano  visi-

                            Petrus  de  Crespy,  dictorum  religiosorum  constitutus  procurator

                            precessorum   et  predecessorum   nostrorum  duodecim   marcas,  pro
