No. 642. [Fourth quarter 13th. century]
1. Ema, daughter of William Armelyn de Ludegodefray;
2. Dominus William de Lude, chaplain.
GRANT in fee-farm, in consideration of three shillings, of half a meadow in the meadow of Ludegodefray extending at one end above the meadow of Robert de Chaundos and at the other end above the meadow of dominus Roger de Burchulle.
Rent: one rose annually.
Witnesses: Miles de Mora, Geoffrey le stede, Gregory Fremon, William son of Martin, William de Lecche, clerk.
Latin.
