HCM 1247
1315, Sunday next after the Feast of St. Peter Apostle called ad Vincula [August 3]. 
1. Thomas son of John de Homptone;
2. Roger de le Halle of the same and Alice, his wife. GRANT of a parcel of pasture in the territory of Homptone, lying in the field called Colcham between the pasture of Walter Arnald' and the land of Walter Cole and extending at one end to the pasture of William le Blake and at the other end towards the Wye. Given at Homptone.
Witnesses: Richard Thorgrim, John de Homptone, Richard Haliday, Nicholes de la Felde, Roger his son.
Latin.
