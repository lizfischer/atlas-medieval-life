April 30, 1276.—Institution to the Vicarage of Yazor  of Simon de 
                 Fekham,  priest, presented by the Priory of Llantony prima. 
                 Mandate  of induction. 
               JAUESAUERE.—Thomas,     etc., Symoni   de Fekham    presbitero, 
           salutem, etc.  Ad  vicariam  ecclesie de Jauesauere vacantem,  ad 
           fresentacionem  Prioris et  Conventus   Lantonie  prime,  verorum 
           patronorum  ejusdem,  [te] admittimus  et canonice  instituimus in 
           eadem.   In cujus, etc. Datum  Londoniis, ij Kalendas Maii. 
               Mandatum    est Officiali quod mittat  eundem   in corporalem 
           possessionem. 

          Fol. 18.
