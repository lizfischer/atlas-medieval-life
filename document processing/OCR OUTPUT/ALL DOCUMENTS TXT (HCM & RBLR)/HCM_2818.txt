HCM 2818
Late 13th. century
LETTER from Ralph, abbot of the monastery of Lyre, to the dean, John [? de Aqua Blanca], and chapter of Hereford, refuting their allegation that the attack on the church of Hereford by frater Hervey, the abbot's proctor in England, Was made with his knowledge and by his authority, and requesting them to exercise patience until he can consult the said Hervey about the actual course of events. latin.
