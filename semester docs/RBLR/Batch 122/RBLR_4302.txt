RBLR 4302
1343.6.19
The bishop of Winchester. On behalf of Roger de Breynton, archdeacon of Gloucester, who has a slender prebend of Hereford, value 10s. that he may accept another, notwithstanding that others by some authority, not that of the pope, expect prebends of the same. Granted. Avignon, 13 Kal. July.
