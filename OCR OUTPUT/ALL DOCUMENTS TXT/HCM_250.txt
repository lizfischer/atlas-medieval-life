HCM 250
1342/3, Tuesdey next efter the Feast of St.
,  Valentine the martyr | February 18]. 1. Richard de Perers} 2. Richerd de Norton’.
 GRANT in fee-farm of twelve acres of land and pasture for four animals in Tunfordmerssh' in Chesthunt and Wormeley which Nicholas Jakeler de Waltham, to whom the said land and pasture descended after the death of Nicholas Saundr', surrendered in grantor's court.
 Rents one rose annually at the Feast of the Nativity of St. John the Baptist. Given at Chesthunt.
 Witnesses: John Bokeler, John Andrewe, John Viker, Richard de Tunford', Henry ‘arles.
 Latin. Seal.
