HCM 2732
1335/6, February 2.
 
 1. Prior and convent of St. Guthlaec of Hereford, appointed sub-collectors in the diocese of Hereford of the tenth for six years lately imposed on the clergy by Pope John XXII for the protection of the Holy Land;
 2. Dean and chapter of the cathedral chureh of Hereford.
 QUITTANCE for twenty-seven pounds ten shillings and seven pence halfpenny in respect of the tenth both of thelr own property and of that of the church of Logwardyn, recently appropriated in the aforesaid diocese, for the first term of the first year. Given at Hereford.
 Latin.
