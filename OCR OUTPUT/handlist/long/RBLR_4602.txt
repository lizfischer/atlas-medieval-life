RBLR 4602
1346.4.22
April 22.-Appointment of Roger de Breynton, archdeacon of Glouces ter, Matthew Northbourch, and William de Herwynton to be the bishop’s proctors on the council of the province. They are instructed to explain the bishop’s absence on the grounds first of deficiency of horses for the journey owing to a sudden epidemic, and secondly of want of money, which from the poverty of the country he is not able even to borrow.
