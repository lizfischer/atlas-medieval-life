Apr. 15—Memorandum     that the sequestration of the fruits of Blaisdon, 
                           due  to the failure of  the Rector to present himself for Holy 
                           Orders, was  released. 

                        RELAXACIO   SEQUESTRI  DE  BLECHEDONE.—Memorandum       quod 
                    Dominus  sequestrum  interpositum in fructibus ecclesie de Bleche- 
                    done, eo  quod  magister A(dam],   Rector ejusdem,  non venit  ad 
                    Ordines, penitus relaxavit; et hoc apud Wyteburne   xvij Kalendas 
                    Maii, anno Domini, etc., octogesimo. 
                                          1—Ebdomada added in MS.
