HCM 3165
1317, December 6.
NOTARIAL INSTRUMENT drawn up and attested by Hugh de Thornhagh', clerk of the diocese of York, notary public, recording the receipt by Roger called Rikeman, friend of of dominus Bartholomew de Batelesmere, knight, from the dean and chapter of Hereford, of one hundred pounds of the tenth for six years granted by Pope Clement V to King Edward II, The said hundred pounds were assigned by the king to the said dominus Bartholomew, The money was delivered to the said Roger in the chapter-house of the cathedral church of Hereford.  Witnesses: domini Robert de Byford', Ranulph de Evesham and Walter de la Bache, vicars in the church of Hereford. Latin. illegible except under ultra-violet rays.
