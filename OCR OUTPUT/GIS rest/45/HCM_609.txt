HCM 609
1307/8, Saturday next before the Feast of the Conversion of St. Paul [January 20].
AGREEMENT between Nicholas de Reygate, clerk, and John son of Roger de Brunsope in which the former agrees to cancel a bond for the payment of sixteen pounds, made before William Godknaue and John Lightfot, clerk of the king for receiving bonds of debt, on condition that he is not disturbed in his rights in the lands which he bought of the said John in Werham with a third part of a dowry which Alice, formerly wife of Richard de Braumford', holds from a free tenement formerly belonging to the said Richard her husband and grandfather of the said John in Werham and which should revert to the said Nicholas on the death of the said Alice. Given at Hereford. Latin.
