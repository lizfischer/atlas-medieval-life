No, 1071. 1342, April 14, NOTARIAL INSTRUMENT drawn up and attested by Philip de la More, clerk of the diocese of Exeter, notary public, in which Thomas de Nonynton' of the diocese of Canterbury, a poor kinsman infra quartum gradum consanguinitatis of dominus Richard, formerly bishop of Hereford, acknowledged the receipt from dominus Roger de Breynton', canon of Hereford and executor of the will of the said bishop, of all that had been bequeathed to him In the will of the said bishop. Mention is made of a clause in the will concerning the poor kinsmen of the bishop. Witnesses: dominus Roger de Hompton' and Robert de Bokenhull', clerks.
