1354, July 107.
. 
 1. Chapter of Hereford, the deanery being vacant;
 2- westid Richard de Staure, vicar of the church of
 ydeneye, John le Whyte, chaplain, end walter de
 Hurste, lessees of the church of lydeneye for five
 years. ,
 REMISSION of four marks at the third annual term in each of the five years on condition that the lessees have faithfully paid the rent due at the first two terms of each year. The annual rent to be paid at three statutory terms is forty pounds. Given at Hereford.
 Latin. Jorn at one corner.
