No. 1004. 1317, Saturday next after the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 14]. RECEIPT from Giles le Ferour to dominus John de Kemeseye, treasurer of Hereford, one of the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for forty shillings sterling bequeathed to the said Giles by dominus Richard in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin.
