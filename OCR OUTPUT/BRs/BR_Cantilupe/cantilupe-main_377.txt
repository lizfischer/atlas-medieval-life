Oct. 4.—Richard  Berner, appearing as  Proctor for David, portionist 
                     of Pontesbury, produced a letter from the Dean of Eastrington 
                     to the effect that David had only the custody of Londesborough. 
                     The  Bishop requiring fuller evidence adjourned the case.
