HCM 2779
1309, Saturday next before the Feast of St. Peter's Ad Vinculs [July 26].
1. Thomas de Orletone; 2. Dean and chepter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford. BOND for the payment at the canons' bakehouse in Hereford of four loads of corn of the value of fourteen shillings each, two loads of oats of the value of twelve shillings each, ond half a load of peas of the value of five shillings in respect of the tithes of the prebend of Hundertone. Given at Hereford. Latin.
