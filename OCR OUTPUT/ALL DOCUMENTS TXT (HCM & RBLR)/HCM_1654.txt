HCM 1654
Late 13th. century
1. Robert de Kynemarsbur'; 2. The monks of St. Peter's, Gloucester.
 GRANT in frank almoign of three acres of woodland lying near the wood which the grantor formerly gave to dominus Reginald, abbot, and the monks of Gloucester.
 Witnesses: Henry le Rues, Ernesius de Brockeworthe, Philip, son of Symon de Mactesdon', William de Okeholte, William Gerald', Roger de Duntesburne.
 Latin.
