Nov. 8.—Mandate  to the Official and the Hebdomadary to install Peter 
                   de Lacy  in the prebend which was held by Giles de Avenbury. 

                P[grrus]  pz  Lacy.—Memorandum      quod  vi Idus Novembris, 
            apud   Chetyntone,  mandabatur   Officiali et Ebdomodario  Here- 
            fordensi quod  sub  alternacione inducerent dominum   Petrum  de 
            Lacy  in corporalem possessionem prebende que  fuit domini Egidii 
            de Avenbury  in Ecclesia Herefordensi; stallum in choro et locum 
            in capitulo, prout moris est, eidem assignantes.
