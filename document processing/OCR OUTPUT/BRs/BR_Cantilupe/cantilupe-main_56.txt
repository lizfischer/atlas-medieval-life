Dec. 22.—Institution to the Church  of Brampton  Bryan  of Robert 
                         de Lacy, subdeacon, presented by the Prior of the Hospital of 
                         St. John of Jerusalem in England.  Mandate  for induction. 

                       Bromprone   Brian.—Thomas,   etc. Roberto  de Lacy, subdiacono, 
                   salutem, etc. Ad  ecclesiam de  Bromptone   Brian vacantem  te, ad 
                   presentacionem religiosi viri fratris Josep de Cauncy,' Prioris Hos- 
                   pitalis Sancti Johannis Jerusalem in  Anglia, veri patroni ejusdem, 
                   admittimus  et canonice instituimus in eadem.  Datum   apud  Lede- 
                   bury, xj Kalendas Januarii, anno predicto. 

                      Mandatum    est Officiali quod mittat eum in corporalem posses- 
                  sionem.
