1316.4.4
No. 33. 1316, April 4. 1. Gilbert de Reygate, clerk; 2. Roger de Breyntone, clerk. GRANT of a messuage and arable land in the town of Breyntone. Given at Breyntone. Witnesses: John de Barewe, then seneschal of the dean and chapter of Hereford, John de la Felde, John de Seuenak', John Pride, Hugh le Schipward', Adam called the clerk, William called the corviser. Latin.
