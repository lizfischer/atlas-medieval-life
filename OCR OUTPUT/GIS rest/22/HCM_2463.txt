HCM 2463
1309/10, Thursday next after the Feast of St. Valentine [February 19].
1. John de Aqua Blanca, dean of the church of St, Ethelbert of Hereford, and the chapter of the same place;
2. Thomas de Henthlan.
 LEASE for one life of twenty acres of land belonging to their church of Bayshan with its chapels, lying the fields of Pencoyt. Annual rent: six shillings and eight pence. Given in the chapter-house at Hereford. Latin. Chipped seal.
