HCM 2585
1306, Sunday next before the Feast of St. Thomas the Apostle [Dec. 18].
1. John de la Barewe, bailiff of the dean and chapter of the church of St. Ethelbert of Hereford; 2. Treasurer of Hereford.
QUITTANCE for twenty shillings in respect of his fee for the term of the Annunciation next following. Given at Hereford.
Latin.
