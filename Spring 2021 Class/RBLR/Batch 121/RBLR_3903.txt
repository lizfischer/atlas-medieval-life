RBLR 3903
1339.4.24
2 23 April [1339] Hartlebury. Memorandum of the receipt at Hartlebury of the archbishop's mandate for enquiry into the detention of the goods of Thomas de Hemenhale, the late bishop [1337-8]. On the following day [24 April] the archdeacons were directed to execute the mandate and to certify what they had done by 14 May.
