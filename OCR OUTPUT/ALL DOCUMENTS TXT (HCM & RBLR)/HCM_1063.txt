HCM 1063
1313, 15 Kal. September [August 18].
COMMISSION from Richard, bishop of Hereford, to the vicar of the church of Woluyuehop' in the diocese of Hereford, to receive in his name the imprisoned clerks on the following Sunday, the next after the Feast of the Assumption of the blessed Mary, before the steward of the dean and chapter of Hereford or his substitute in the manor or liberty of the said dean and chapter at Woluyuehop'. Given at Bosebur'. Latin. Mutilated seal.
