HCM 2719
1307, October.
1. John de Bromfeld', clerk;
2. Dean and chapter of Hereford, by the hand of William the Young. 
QUITTANCE for sixteen shillings and eight pence in respect of fee, viz. half a mark for the term of the Annunciation, 1307, and ten shillings for the term of St. Michael next following. Given at Hereford.
Latin.
