 ORDINANCE of Bishop Thomas .Cherlton] providing that the revenues of Lugwardine, which, with its eee chapelries, had lately passed under the control of the dean and chapter, should henceforth, after due provision for the maintenance of a vicar, be used for the provision of Vicars choral in the cathedral church. The residue is to pass to the fabric fund of the cathedral church.
