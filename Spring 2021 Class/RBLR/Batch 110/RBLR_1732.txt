No. 1003. 1317, Wednesday next after the Feast of the Ascension of our Lord [May 18]. RECEIPT from Richard de Wynchecoumbe to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty shillings bequeathed to him by the said dominus Richard in his will. Given at Hereford. Latin .
