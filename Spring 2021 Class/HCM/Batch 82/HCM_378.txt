HCM 378
1317, Monday next after the Feast of St. Germanus (May 23?).
1. Henry de Heyton', clerk; 2. Richard the smith of Neuton'. RELEASE of a messuage formerly of Robert de la Sete in Neuton', lying between the tenement of William Bene and the tenement formerly of Hugh Rondulf. Given at Lodelawe. Witnesses: Richard de Brome, Richard Gilbard, William the clerk of Stok', William Bene, Richard Le Vikerys. Latin.
