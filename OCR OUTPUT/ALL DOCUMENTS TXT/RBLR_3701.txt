RBLR 3701
1337.1.12
Jan. 12. Tower of London. Licence for the alienation in mortmain by Roger de Breynton, prebendary of Norton in the church of St. Ethelbert, Hereford, to the vicars of that church of two messuages, a virgate and a half of land, 4 acres of meadow, and 10s. of rent in Breynton and Upton, to celebrate divine service daily there according to his appointment for his soul and the souls of William de Breynton and Nicholas de Reygate, late treasurer of the church, their fathers and mothers and the faithful departed. By fine of 40s. Hereford.
