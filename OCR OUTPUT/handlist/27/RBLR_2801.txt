No. 1051. 1327/8, March 5. LETTERS PATENT of Adam, bishop of Hereford, recognising the justice of the administration of the will of dominus Richard, formerly bishop of Hereford, by the executors domini John de la Felde and Roger de Breintone, canons of the church of Hereford, and absolving them from all further responsibility. A letter from the archbishop of Canterbury, dated 31 July 1320, is also quoted, granting authority for the above action to the said Adam, bishop of Hereford. Given at London. Latin.
