HCM 530
1297, Sunday next after the Feast of Ste
  John snte nortem latina (May 12).
 LETTERS OF ATTORNEY from EE John de Marewent, clerk, to Robert Pippard snd Walter de Bannebury empowering then bo give seisin to Henry de Marewent, brother of the said John, of one messuage, arable land and meadow which the seid Sohn had of the grent of Emma his sister and one wife of Robert son of Richard of Byshampton' in the vill of Byshampton', Given at Redeford.
 Latin.
