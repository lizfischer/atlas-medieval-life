June  27.—A   writ, ordering postponement in the collection of scutage, 
                   was  given to W.  de Redmarley,   to be handed  to the Sheriff 
                   of Hereford  in  case he should  distrain for the same. _ The 
                   writ was not  transcribed. 

                Pro   RESPECTU   scuTAGII.—Memorandum      quod   die  Martis 
            proximo  post Nativitatem  Beati Johannis  Baptiste, apud Arleye, 
            traditum fuit domino  W.  de Rudmerleye   quoddam   breve Domini 
            Regis, Vicecomiti Herefordie per  ipsum  tradendum,  de  respectu 
           scutagii habendo.  Tamen   ipsum breve dicto Vicecomiti non deberet 
            tradi nisi ipse faceret districcionem pro scutagio memorato; cujus 
            brevis transcriptum tune nullatenus habebatur.
