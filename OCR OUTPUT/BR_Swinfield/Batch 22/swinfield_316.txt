July  23.—Richard   de  Pudlestone  submits  himself unreservedly  to 
                                       the bishop's pleasure, and formally  resigns all claim  to the 
                                       canonry and  prebend procured  by papal  provision to the dis- 
                                      regard  of his earlier pledges and  the bishop's interests.
