No. 1014. 1317/8, Thursday next after the Feast of Saint Benedict, the abbot [March 23] RECEIPT from Michael de Berehem, canon in the church of Hereford, to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for one gold ring which dominus Robert de Borgat’, formerly abbot of Radyng’, gave to the said dominus Richard, and which the said dominus Richard bequeathed Michael de Bereham in his will. Given at Canterbury. Latin.
