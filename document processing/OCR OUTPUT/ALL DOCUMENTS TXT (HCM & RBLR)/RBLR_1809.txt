No. 1039. 1317/8, Thursday next after the Feast of Saint Benedict the abbot [March 23]. RECEIPT from the Master and priests of the hospitalof the priests of Canterbury to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty shillings which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to them in his will. Given at Canterbury. Latin. Seal broken at the edges.
