Dean and chapter of Hereford, RESIGNATION of the church of Dudelbur’ which om held at farm, and of the church of Longestanton’ they received from the dean and chapter for an annual fee-farm rent of thirty-five marks according to the agreement made between ae John, ee of the said prior, and the conv and the dean and chapter.
