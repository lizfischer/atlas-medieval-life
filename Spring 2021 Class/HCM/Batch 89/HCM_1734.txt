HCM 1734
1317, 8 Ides October [October 8].
1, William de Preston’; 2. Hugh de Sothynton', clerk,
 GRANT in fee-farm of all his land with the buildings erected upon it and all its appurtenances lying in New Street (novus vicus) in the vill of Ledebur' between the land of Randolphe Berde and the land of the grantor. Rent: twelve pence annually at four specified terms to the Hospital of the Blessed Katherine at Ledbury, Given at Ledbury. Witnesses: Walter de Weston’, Richard berde, Ralph le Botiler, Giles le Ferur, Roger Pelliparie. Latin.
