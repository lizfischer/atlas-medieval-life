HCM 689
1285, the Feast of the Pentecost [May 13].
1. William Payn de Deuswelle; 2. William the forester.
 LEASE for twelve years, in consideration of four shillings and six pence, of one acre of arable land in Deuswelle called le Pikedacr' lying between the land formerly of Henry de Baddesaghe and the land formerly of Cradoc Seisel and extending at one end to Lonwecoppe and at the other to the land of the said Cradoc Seysel.
 Witnesses: William de la Calewe, John Buter, John ad fontem, William Mangaunt, Adam the medicus, Hugh Helle, Stephen the dyer, William de Locche (sic.), clerk. Latin.
