HCM 1049
1314, Wednesday next before the Feast of the Nativity of the Blessed John the Baptist [June 19].
RECEIPT from Richard, bishop of Hereford, to dominus John de Kemeseye numerans for twenty pounds received from the dean and chapter of Hereford on Monday following the Feast of Saint Barnabas the apostle at Hereford in the same year, in part payment of two hundred marks in which the said dean and chapter were held by bond.
Given at Bosebur'.
Latin.
