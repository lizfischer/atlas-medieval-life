Memorandum.—The     right of giving in marriage the heirs of William 
                           Marmion    is transferred to the Bishop for 250  marks.   Their 
                           wardship   was offered to him for 300  marks, but not accepted. 
                           For  the estate, till the heirs were of age, he offered £100.
