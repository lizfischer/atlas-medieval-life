HCM 1611
1342, Tuesday the Feast of St. Barnabas the Apostle | June 11j.
1. Abbot and convent of St. Peter's, Gloucester; 2. Geoffrey de Sotton' and Isabella his wife.
 LEASE for seventy years, of a messuage with a piece of land sdjoining end its appurtenances in Bromfeld' situated between the cemetery of the parish church of Bromfeld' on the south and the stream of Onye on the north. The annual rent to be paid to the prior of Bromfeld' is four pence at tw specified terms. The lessees shall also render their best animal or forty pence as a heriot as the prior of Bromfeld' shall determine. Right of re-entry is reserved to the lessors in the event of non-psyment of rent or other irregularity. Given in the chapter-house at Gloucester,
 Latin.
