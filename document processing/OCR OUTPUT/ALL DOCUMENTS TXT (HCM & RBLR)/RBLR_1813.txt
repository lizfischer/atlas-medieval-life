No. 1027. 1317/8, Friday the next after the Feast of Saint Benedict the abbot [March 24]. RECEIPT from Thomas de Coleshulle to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for a hundred shillings which the said dominus Richard bequeathed to him in his will. Given at Wyngeham. Latin. Two seals, one with broken edges.
