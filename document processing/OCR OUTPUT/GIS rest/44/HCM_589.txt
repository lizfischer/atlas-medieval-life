HCM 589
1311, Thursday next after the Feast of St. Margaret the Virgin [July 22].
1. Walter de la Stepple; 
2. Domini Walter de Thornbury, William his brother, and Isabel wife of the said William.
RELEASE of lands and tenements in Yeddefen loges. Given et Herdewyk'.
Witnesses: John de la More, Richard le Schep, Adam de Sned, William de Bleegh(?), John de Banwelle.
Latin.
