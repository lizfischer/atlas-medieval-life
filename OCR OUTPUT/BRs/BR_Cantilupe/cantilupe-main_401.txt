Feb. 23.—Collation of John  de Clare to the Church of Colwall, and 
                         mandate of induction sent to the Dean of Frome. 

                      CoLLACIO  ECCLESIE  DE  CoLEWELLE.—Item    eo tempore  contulit 
                  Dominus   ecclesiam de Colewelle domino  J [ohanni] de Clare, cari- 
                  tativo intuitu. Et tunc Decano  de Froma  extitit demandatum quod 
                  eum  induceret, etc.
