Feb. 22.—Mandate  of induction to the custody of Humber Church of 
                John  de Cava, presented by Thomas Costentyn and his wife 
                Joanna. 
              Humepe.—X     Kalendas  Marcii mandatum   est Officiali quod 
          inducat Johannem de Cava, presentatum per Thomam   Costentyn et 
          Johannam   uxorem ejus ad  capellam de Humbre,  in possessionem 
           ejusdem capelle nomine custodie.
