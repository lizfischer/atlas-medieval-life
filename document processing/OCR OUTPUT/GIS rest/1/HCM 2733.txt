No. 2733. 1338, circa March 25.

1. Nicholas de Merkynton, executor of the will of magister Henry de Shorn, canon of the cathedral church of Hereford;
2. Dean and chapter of Hereford.

RECEIPT for forty-four pounds in full payment of an annual pension of fifty pounds payable for the term of the Annunciation. Given at Hereford.

Latin.
