HCM 1395
Late 13th. century
1. Thomas de Gines; 2. Thomas le Greye de Halbeton.
 RELEASE, in consideration of ten marks, of all that land which dominus John le Brutun, then bishop of Hereford, granted to him for his service in the vili of Colewell', and which Bilbert Barri formerly held of the said bishop of Hereford.
 Witnesses: William the chamberlain de Maveinto', Richard de Middelton, Adam (?) de Cheveha' William de Schireburne, Richard Ruddue, William Fillel.
 Latin.536.
