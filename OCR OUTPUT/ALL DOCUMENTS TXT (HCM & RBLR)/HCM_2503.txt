HCM 2503
Late 14th century Thursday, in the Vigil of St. Luke the Evangelist 
PRESENTMENT of the jurors in the manor of Preston' on la laweday, namely Philip Dypre, Adam de Muriual, William de godeweye, John de Kynley, Adam de lolham Roger de la hide, Walter Tibote, Roger kant, Richard Willames, William son of Juliana, Adam de childestone, Walter le Waleys de la Bache, Adam le franceis, Henry de la ford, and Robert kant, who gave it as their verdict that John, son of Adam de la felde, set fire to the house of Hugh de Ploufeld', namely his grange, by the assent and consent of Alice, wife of John Hake. Latin. Twelve fragmentary seals.554.
