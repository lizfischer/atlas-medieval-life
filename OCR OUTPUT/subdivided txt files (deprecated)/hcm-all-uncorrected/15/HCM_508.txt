Late 13th century
1. Ema Rord formerly wife of Thomas son of John formerly citizen of Hereford, widow; 2. William Jonderay citizen of Hereford. RELEASE of a shop formerly belonging to the said Thomas son of John in the ery of Hereford lying between the shop formerly of Walter Ingian and the shop formerly of John Cays.
