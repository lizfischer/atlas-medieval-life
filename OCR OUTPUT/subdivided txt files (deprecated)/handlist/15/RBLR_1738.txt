No. 1007. 1317, Wednesday next after the Feast of the Translation of the blessed Thomas the martyr [July 13]. RECEIPT from Adam de Cranebrok' and Gunnora de Boynton', his wife, to the executors of the will of dominus Richard de Swynefeud, formerly bishop of Hereford, for twenty marks bequeathed to them by the said dominus Richard in his will. Given at London and Wyngeham. Latin. Seal.
