1337. Dec. 30.  Guildford. Presentation of Roger de Braynton, archdeacon of Gloucester, to the church of Old Radenore in the diocese of Hereford, in the king's gift by reason of his custody of the lands and heir of Edmund de Mortuo Mari, tenant in chief; on an exchange of benefices with Bartholomew Tirel, king's clerk. By p.s.
