No. 3163. 1342, April 9. NOTARIAL INSTRUMENT drawn up and attested by Philipp de la More, clerk of the diocese of Exeter, notary public, recording a receipt given by Alianora, wife of Roger Racy of Bosebury, and Richard, Roger, Juliana, and Alice, her children, through their proctors the said Roger Racy and Richard de Aust, to dorminus Roger de Breynton', canon of Hereford, for the legacies bequeathed to them by do minus Richard de Swynesfeld', late bishop of Hereford, who was their kinsman. witnesses: John de Munstreworth' and John de Stanford'. Latin.
