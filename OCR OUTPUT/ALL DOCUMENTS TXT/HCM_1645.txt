1297-98 26 Edward I.
, 
 LETTER of Edwerd LI] to the abbot and convent of Gloucester es that certain abbots and convents in his kingdom had, ot the king's request, made out letters obligatory for certain sums of money to Coppus Joseph and other merchants to obtein the release of John of Ste John who was detained in a French prison. The king requests the abbot and convent of Gloucester to make similar ; letters obligatory for the seme purpose. The king will preserve them harmless from the said merchantse The abbot and convent are to inform the king by the bearer of this letter of the action they propose to take. Given at Ste Edmunds.
 Latin. Illeeible in parts.
