                         Abberley, on the presentation of William  de  St. Omer  and 
                         Petronella his wife. Mandate of induction. 

                      INsTITUCIO   ECCLESIE  DE   ABBEDELEYE.—Item     memorandum

                  stitucionem  in ecclesia de  Aubedele, ad  presentacionem  domini 
                  Willelmi de  Sancto Audomaro   et  Petronelle uxoris ejusdem.  Et
